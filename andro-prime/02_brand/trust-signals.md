# Trust Signals

> **Status: DRAFT — compiled 2026-07-02 from already-ratified wording.** Owner: `02_brand`. **Any new or changed trust claim must clear `03_compliance` / Ewa before customer-facing use.** The §2 long-form wording is an approved *direction* whose final phrasing is pending Ewa sign-off (see below); everything else here is already ratified.

This file collects the approved forms of Andro Prime's credibility claims so trust language stays consistent and never drifts into a regulatory claim.

**Governing rule:** a trust claim must never imply Andro Prime **diagnoses**, that a clinician **reviews an individual customer's results**, or that **TRT is available now**. Phase 0 is a wellness information service with no per-customer clinical interpretation. Source of the clinical-governance position: `../03_compliance/clinical-governance-position.md` (Ewa-approved 2026-05-22).

---

## 1. Lab accreditation (Vitall / UKAS)

**Approved forms:**

- "Vitall (UKAS ISO 15189 accredited)"
- "UKAS ISO 15189 Accredited Lab"
- "Analysed by a UKAS ISO 15189 accredited laboratory"

**Use:** kit / supplement / results pages, partner and affiliate briefs. Vitall is the testing partner (legal entity Healthy Human Labs Ltd); the accreditation belongs to the **lab**.

**Do not:** imply Andro Prime itself is UKAS-accredited (it is not — the lab is), or that accreditation means clinical oversight/sign-off of an individual's results.

---

## 2. Clinical governance (Dr Ewa Lindo, GMC-registered GP)

The sensitive one. Ewa signs off the results **system** (bands, thresholds, marker explanations, safety-netting) — **not individual customers' results**. All clinical-credibility copy is governed by the verb test.

**Verb test (from `clinical-governance-position.md`, Ewa-approved 2026-05-22):**

- ✅ **SAFE — authorship of the system, past tense:** a GP **set / designed / developed / wrote / signed off / approved** the report, the ranges, the explanations.
- ⛔ **NOT SAFE — an act on the customer:** a GP **reviews / has reviewed / interprets / checks / looks at / assesses** your results.
- Customer-specific outputs the report produces ("your recommendation", "what your result means", "where your number sits") remain fine.

**Ratified short form (CA-001/003 re-sweep, 2026-05-26):**

- "recommendation logic signed off by a GMC-registered GP"

**Approved long form — direction approved 2026-05-22; final wording pending Ewa sign-off:**

> "A GMC-registered GP, Dr Ewa Lindo, set how every Andro Prime result works: the healthy ranges your numbers are measured against, the plain-English explanation of each marker, and the recommendation you receive. It is clear, GP-built information about your own data. It is not a one-to-one consultation or a personal medical opinion."

**Contact-page template (live wording):**

> "Dr Ewa Lindo designs and signs off the results interpretation framework and the results report copy; Andro Prime does not offer one-to-one clinical consultations; where a result needs medical attention the customer is told clearly and directed to their GP."

⛔ **Prohibited (misleading-claim / Phase-0-boundary risk):** "Dr Ewa Lindo reviews every result", "has reviewed your results", "reviewed by a GMC-registered doctor", "GP-interpreted report", "GMC-registered prescriber", "GP-built report" (per-patient framing), or any implication TRT is available now.

---

## 3. Convenience / access

**Approved forms:** "No GP needed", "5 minutes", "at-home finger-prick".

**Do not:** pair "No GP needed" with anything implying Andro Prime replaces clinical care.

---

## 4. Do-not list (applies to all trust copy)

- No "diagnose / diagnosis / treat / treatment / cure".
- No implication a clinician reviews an individual's results (see the verb test).
- No TRT-availability claim in Phase 0 ("be first when we launch", never "available now").
- No claim that Andro Prime is UKAS-accredited (the lab is).
- Ashwagandha stays silent — never used as a trust or credibility point.
