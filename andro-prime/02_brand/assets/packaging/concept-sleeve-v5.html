Print spec (give this to the printer)

  • Sleeve flat / trim: 179 × 304 mm, folds to two faces of 179 × 152 mm (back face rotated 180° on the flat). Add 3 mm bleed all round; keep text ≥5 mm inside trim.
  • White compliance window: reserved on the back face — Vitall prints lot / expiry / barcode / kit ID into it. Exact rectangle (size + position) still to be lifted from the dieline — the one shown is approximate.
  • Insert card: A6 (105 × 148 mm) proposed — confirm it fits the kit interior.
  • Finish = the premium lever: heavy uncoated stock, soft-touch matte, debossed AP monogram on the sleeve; the insert as a black card with white or gunmetal foil. (Print direct with a premium printer; don't use Vitall's facilitated printing if you want these finishes.)
  • MOQ 500 sleeves, kitting FOC; ~1 month setup.

Compliance / copy still to clear

  • Insert copy is DRAFT pending Ewa / compliance sign-off before print (it stays inside approved framing: "GP-standard thresholds approved by a GMC-registered GP" — no bespoke-interpretation claim).
  • Collection steps are deliberately NOT on the insert — they remain on Vitall's validated IFU (which can carry your logo). The insert only points to it.
  • Wording uses "activate", not "register" — the kit is pre-linked at order creation.
