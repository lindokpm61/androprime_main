# Andro Prime — Kit Packaging: Print Production Brief (v1)

**Owner:** Keith Antony / Andro Prime Ltd · **Date:** 2026-06-12 · **Status:** Draft for quotation
**Purpose:** Brief a print supplier to quote and produce the Andro Prime kit packaging. Two printed items: an outer **sleeve** and an inner **welcome insert card**. This is for quotation and proofing only — do **not** proceed to a full print run until the items marked **[TBC]** are confirmed and the insert copy is signed off (see §9).

---

## 1. Context (how the packaging is used)

Andro Prime sells premium at-home blood-test kits (£99–£179). Fulfilment is white-label: our lab partner (Vitall) assembles and dispatches the physical kit. We supply our **own outer sleeve** (which Vitall slides over the kit) and our **own welcome insert** (which Vitall places inside). We do **not** control the kit's interior tray or the validated collection-instruction sheet (that stays with the lab). So all of our brand and reassurance work lives on these two printed pieces.

The design intent is **premium and reassuring**: a clean warm-white sleeve, with the "shout" of premium delivered by material/finish and by a black foil insert. Strictly **black and white only** — no colour, gradient, or rounded corners.

## 5. Materials & finishes (the premium lever)

The premium read comes from stock and finish, not from the artwork. Please quote the recommended route and the alternative for each item.

### A. Sleeve — warm-white
- **Recommended:** heavyweight carton board **≈ 350–400 gsm**, **soft-touch matte laminate** outer, with a **blind deboss of the AP monogram** on the front. Optional **spot-gloss (spot-UV) on the monogram** as an alternative to deboss.
- **Alternative (natural route):** uncoated natural/ivory board ≈ 350–400 gsm, no laminate, black ink + blind deboss (warmth carried by the stock itself).
- Square-fold, sharp creases, no rounded corners.

### B. Insert — black
- **Recommended:** **black-throughout uncoated/duplex board ≈ 400 gsm**; body text in **opaque white** (screen-print or digital white); the **AP monogram and the headline in foil** (matte white or gunmetal); optional blind deboss on the monogram.
- **Alternative (cost route):** printed black + soft-touch matte on a white board, white reversed-out text, foil monogram.
- Note the white-ink/foil requirement on black stock drives cost — please quote both routes.
