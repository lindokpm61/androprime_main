# Keith Antony — Video Short Transcript (Canonical Voice Sample)

Short video script / spoken transcript covering the same idea as LinkedIn Post 1 in [linkedin-posts-keith-antony.md](./linkedin-posts-keith-antony.md). Different format, same DNA — fragment-dense, triadic rhythm, the reframe pivot intact.

This is the **compressed end** of Keith's voice register. Approximately 4:1 compression vs the LinkedIn version of the same idea.

---

A founder told me their team
keeps making expensive mistakes.

Every quote has to go through them.
Every invoice.
Every proposal.

I asked one question.

Where's the pricing framework?

The margins.
The discount rules.
The walk-away number.

Silence.

There wasn't one.

Twelve people in the business.
The entire commercial logic
lived in one place.
The founder's head.

Staff weren't making mistakes.
They were guessing.

No framework.
No criteria.
No authority boundaries.

Every time they got it wrong,
it confirmed what the founder believed.

"I can't trust them with this."

So the checking got worse.
And the team learned to wait
rather than decide.

That's not a trust problem.
That's an INSTRUMENTATION problem.

You're running a 15-person business
with a 5-person nervous system.

Follow for more.
