## Long version (affiliate briefs, press, About page)

Andro Prime is a UK men's health company built on a simple inversion: your health data should belong to you, not sit in a clinic's filing system.

Go private the conventional way and the clinic holds your record. You get a number read out at an appointment, a follow-up booking, and very little you can actually keep or compare later. Andro Prime does the opposite. You see everything we see. Your results, in your own dashboard, in plain English, tracked over time as you retest, and yours to keep whether or not you ever buy anything else from us.

Underneath that is one method, applied every time: blood data before any recommendation. Not a guess. Not a generic protocol based on age and symptoms. Your actual numbers.

Most men who come to us have been to their GP. They've been told their results are normal. And they still feel terrible: exhausted, slow to recover, not themselves. The problem isn't that their GP is wrong. It's that "normal" means you're not clinically deficient. It doesn't mean you're optimal. There's a meaningful gap between those two things, and that's where most men over 35 are living.

We close that gap with an at-home finger-prick blood test. You order it, take the sample in five minutes at home, post it back in the prepaid envelope. Results come back in 48 hours through your personal dashboard, in plain English, with a specific recommendation based on your numbers. Not a lab reference table. Not a suggestion to go and see your GP.

Phase Zero is the wellness tier: three diagnostic kits covering testosterone, energy and recovery markers, and a full six-marker panel. Where your results show a deficiency, we recommend a supplement we actually manufacture, one that contains exactly the ingredient your blood says you need, at the dose the evidence supports. If your testosterone comes back below 12 nmol/L, you can join the founding member list and be first to hear when we launch clinical TRT.

The clinical tier (TRT, peptide therapy, and ongoing monitoring under Dr Ewa Lindo, GMC-registered and Harley Street TRT-trained) follows once CQC registration is complete. The wellness tier funds operations, proves the model, and builds the future clinical pipeline before we open the clinical doors.

This is the only UK men's health brand that runs the full loop and hands you the record at the end of it: test, interpret, recommend, supply, retest. Your data first, every time. Yours to keep.
