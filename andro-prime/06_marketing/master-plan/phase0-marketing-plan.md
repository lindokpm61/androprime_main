## 1. Executive Summary

Andro Prime is a UK men's health company built on a single founding insight: **the data should come before the recommendation**. Every kit, supplement, and clinical service flows from a blood result — not a generic product catalogue.

**The marketing objective for Phase 0** is to build the wellness tier into a self-sustaining profit centre that funds operations, validates product-market fit, and creates the pre-qualified patient pipeline for the clinical tier post-CQC.

**The acquisition model is affiliate-first, ads-second.** Trust is borrowed from established voices before it is paid for through cold advertising. This reduces CAC, accelerates time to first revenue, and creates social proof that makes subsequent paid campaigns more efficient.

**Website architecture note for Phase 0:** The public site should remain connected for trust and branded/organic discovery, but paid, affiliate, and retargeting traffic should be routed to reduced-navigation `/lp/` variants built around a single CTA.

**Product:** Kit 2 — Energy & Recovery Check (£44)

### Campaign 2: "The GP Said Normal" (ICP 1 Awareness)

**Product:** Kit 1 — Testosterone Health Check (£29)

**Core message:** Normal isn't optimal. Find out where you actually stand.

**Creative hook options:**
- *"Your GP said your testosterone is normal. Here's why that might not be the full story."*

**Note on Google Search for Campaign 2:**
The terms `GP refused testosterone` and `testosterone borderline range` are high-intent, low-competition terms that Voy and Medichecks are not effectively targeting. These are the highest purchase-intent terms in the category and should be prioritised in bid strategy.

**Core message:** TRT is coming. Be first. Lock your slot. No risk.
**Trigger:** This campaign does not run publicly. It activates through the post-result email sequence for men whose Kit 1 or Kit 3 result shows testosterone < 12 nmol/L.

## 8. Channel Execution Plans

### 8.1 Google Search Advertising

**Account structure:**

```
Campaign 1: Kit 2 (Energy & Recovery)
  Ad Group 1: Fatigue/tiredness terms
    → why am I always tired male
    → men's energy blood test UK
    → fatigue blood test at home
  Ad Group 2: Recovery terms
    → slow recovery after gym
    → joint inflammation blood test

Campaign 2: Kit 1 (Testosterone)
  Ad Group 1: Direct testosterone terms
    → testosterone test at home UK
    → private testosterone blood test
  Ad Group 2: NHS frustration terms
    → GP refused testosterone test
    → testosterone borderline range UK

Campaign 3: Kit 3 (Hormone & Recovery Check)
  Ad Group 1: Men's health check terms
    → men's health MOT
    → comprehensive blood test men UK
```

**Ad copy principles:**
- Always lead with the symptom, not the product
- Include price in headline (reduces unqualified clicks)
- Mention lab accreditation in description (trust signal)
- CTA: "Test at Home" not "Buy Now"

**Example ad — Kit 2:**
```
Headline 1: Energy & Recovery Blood Test — £44
Headline 2: Find Out Why You're Tired & Sore
Headline 3: UKAS Accredited Lab | Results in 72hrs
Description 1: Test 4 key markers from home. No GP.
              Personalised results with supplement recommendations.
Description 2: Vitamin D, Magnesium, CRP, Ferritin.
              Know exactly what your body needs. Free UK delivery.
```

**Negative keywords (apply from Day 1):**
- free, nhs, symptoms of, what is, how to, reddit, forum, diagnosis

**Bid strategy:**
- Start: Manual CPC with £2–4 max bids
- Month 2+: Switch to Target CPA once 20+ conversions tracked
- Target CPA: £35–45

## 9. Email Strategy & CRM

**Platform:** Customer.io — event-first, triggered by Stripe and Thriva webhooks. Sequences are defined as behavioural campaigns fired by lifecycle events emitted from the application. Do not use Klaviyo or Mailchimp for this build.

### List Segments

| Segment | Trigger | Sequence |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Pre-launch interest | Waitlist sign-up | seq-01 |
| Kit buyer — result pending | Kit purchase confirmed (Stripe webhook) | seq-02 |
| Kit buyer — result delivered (deficiency) | Result: low D, low Mg, low B12, or elevated CRP | seq-03a |
| Kit buyer — result delivered (low T) | Result: T < 12 nmol/L from Kit 1 or Kit 3 | seq-03b |
| Supplement subscriber | First subscription payment (Stripe webhook) | seq-04 |
| Supplement subscriber — at risk | No engagement 45+ days | seq-05 |
| Founding member depositor | Deposit payment | seq-03b (founding member branch) |
