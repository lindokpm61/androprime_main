## Strategic Overview

### The Core Principle: Borrow Trust, Then Buy It

The standard DTC launch playbook — spend heavily on cold paid ads, build brand over time — is structurally disadvantaged for Andro Prime at launch because:

1. **New health brands have no earned trust.** Cold audiences don't buy a £44 blood test from a company they've never heard of at useful conversion rates.
2. **Funded competitors dominate paid acquisition.** Voy has a seven-figure ad budget. Medichecks has decade-long SEO authority. Bidding against them on cold traffic from Day 1 is expensive and slow.
3. **Kit margins don't absorb high CAC.** At £12–22 gross profit per kit, a £50+ cold CAC is immediately loss-making without supplement conversion LTV.

**The alternative:** Start where trust already exists. Deploy into communities where the ICP already spends time, through voices they already trust. Affiliates create the warm audience; paid ads close it.

**Page architecture implication:** Phase 0 traffic should not be sent to browse-heavy canonical product pages. The recommended structure is a connected public site for trust and discovery plus reduced-navigation `/lp/` variants for paid, affiliate, and retargeting traffic. Those LP variants should follow a single-CTA rule.

## Pre-Launch (Weeks -6 to 0)

Everything in this phase is preparation. No kits are sold. No ads are running. This period exists to ensure that on launch day, the trust infrastructure is already in place.

### Week -6 to -4: Influencer Identification & Outreach

**Target creator profile:**
- UK-based (essential — geo-targeting matters for NHS messaging)
- Male audience (80%+)
- Content category: men's fitness, health optimisation, biohacking, 40+ lifestyle, PT/coaching
- Follower range: 10,000–150,000 (micro and mid-tier; better engagement than macro)
- Engagement rate: >2%
- Not currently sponsored by a direct competitor

**How to find them:**
- Instagram search: `#menshealthuk`, `#mensfitnessuk`, `#bloodtest`, `#biohackinguk`, `#over40fitness`
- YouTube: search "testosterone UK", "men's health check", "blood test results" — filter to UK creators
- Tools: Modash, Heepsy, or manual Instagram prospecting (free)

**Outreach volume:** Contact 40–60 creators to secure 15–25 active affiliates.

**Outreach message (DM or email):**

> Hi [Name], I've been watching your content for a while — your take on [specific video/post] was exactly right. I'm launching Andro Prime, a men's health brand in the UK built around blood testing and data-driven supplementation. Unlike generic test kits, our results tell you exactly what your body needs — and why.
>
> I'd love to send you one of our Energy & Recovery kits completely free, no strings. Test it. Film what you want. If you like it and think your audience would benefit, we have an affiliate programme — 20% commission on every sale through your link. If you don't like it, no worries — honest feedback is just as valuable.
>
> Interested?

### Week -6 to -2: Personal Trainer Affiliate Programme

**Why PTs are the highest-leverage channel:**
- A PT who understands biomarker-led training will recommend Kit 2 to every client experiencing fatigue or slow recovery
- It makes their service better
- It costs them nothing and earns them commission
- 50 active PT affiliates referring 4 clients/month = 200 kit sales/month at ~£9 CAC

**Target PT profile:**
- UK-based freelance or studio PTs
- Specialises in: 35+ men, body composition, sports performance, rehabilitation
- Active on Instagram or LinkedIn
- Has a client base of 15+ active clients

**How to find them:**
- Instagram: search `#personaltraineruk`, `#londonPT`, `#manchesterPT`, etc.
- LinkedIn: search "personal trainer UK" — filter by location
- PT associations: CIMSPA directory, REPs (Register of Exercise Professionals)

**Outreach volume:** Contact 300–500 PTs. Target 50+ active affiliates by launch.

**Outreach message:**

> Hi [Name], quick question for you. Do your clients ever come to you exhausted, recovering slowly, or just not performing the way they used to?
>
> I'm launching Andro Prime — a men's health brand that uses blood testing to find out exactly what's holding someone back (Vitamin D, Magnesium, inflammation markers, testosterone, etc.). The results give a personalised supplement recommendation.
>
> I'd like to offer you a free kit to try yourself, and if you find it useful, an affiliate arrangement: 20% commission on every client who tests through your unique link. For context, that's £8.80 per Kit 2 sale — for a recommendation that takes you 30 seconds.
>
> Happy to jump on a quick call if useful.

**Affiliate structure:**
- Each PT gets a unique discount code (e.g., `PT-SMITH20`) they can share
- 15% discount to client, 20% commission to PT (cost to Andro Prime: 35% of kit price)
- Tracked via Refersion, PartnerStack, or GoAffPro (Shopify-compatible)
- Monthly commission payments via bank transfer

> The 35% combined cost (discount + commission) on Kit 2 (£44) = £15.40. Kit 2 gross profit after COGS is £22. Net after affiliate cost = £6.60 per referred kit. Positive on the kit alone, before supplement conversion LTV.

### Week -4 to -1: Keith's LinkedIn Foundation

**Minimum viable presence before launch:**
- LinkedIn profile fully completed with Andro Prime in role
- 3–5 posts already published:
  - Why Keith started Andro Prime (founder story)
  - "My GP told me my testosterone was normal. He was right. Here's why it still wasn't enough."
  - "What 6 biomarkers tell you about your health that your GP doesn't test"
  - A post about the NHS gap in men's testosterone care
  - A post introducing Ewa and why her credentials matter

**Posting cadence from launch:** 3× per week minimum.

### Week -2 to 0: Paid Infrastructure Setup

Before launch, set up but do not activate:

**Google Search:**
- Campaign structure: separate campaigns for Kit 1 terms, Kit 2 terms, Kit 3 terms
- Match types: phrase and exact only — no broad on a new account
- Negative keywords: "free", "NHS", "symptoms of" (information intent, not purchase intent)
- Location: UK only

**Meta Ads:**
- Create Business Manager, pixel, and ad account
- Install Meta pixel on website before launch (collects visitor data immediately)
- Do NOT run ads yet — let the pixel collect data first

**Affiliate tracking:**
- Set up GoAffPro or Refersion
- Create unique discount codes for all confirmed PT and influencer affiliates before launch
- Test tracking end-to-end with a dummy order before going live

## Launch (Weeks 1–4)

### Week 1: Go Live

**Day 1:**
- All three kits go live simultaneously
- Google Search campaigns activate: Kit 1 terms, Kit 2 terms, Kit 3 terms
- PT and influencer affiliates notified their codes are live
- Keith publishes LinkedIn announcement post
- Founding member programme goes live alongside Kit 1

**Week 1–2:**
- Monitor Google Search daily: pause any keywords with >5 clicks and 0 conversions after 10+ clicks
- CAC ceiling: if any campaign exceeds £55 CAC after 50 clicks, pause and rebuild ad copy
- Influencer content: aim for 5–8 creators posting in Week 1–3 (staggered is fine)
- Do not run Meta ads yet

**Target Week 1–4 outcomes:**
- 30–60 kit sales (primarily from Google Search + influencer affiliate)
- 5–10 founding member deposits (from Kit 1 low-T results)
- Pixel collecting data from all visitors
- 2–5 influencer posts live with authentic kit experience content

### Week 4: Activate Meta Warm Retargeting

By Week 4, the pixel has 2–4 weeks of visitor data. Warm audiences that now exist:

| Audience | Expected size by Week 4 |
|----------|------------------------|
| Website visitors | 500–2,000 |
| Kit page viewers | 200–800 |
| Influencer engaged | 1,000–5,000 |
| Email list | 50–100 |

**Retargeting ads by audience:**

- **Website visitors who didn't buy:** "Still wondering why you're knackered all the time? Your results are waiting." → Kit 2
- **Kit page viewers:** "[X] men tested this month. Average result: 2 deficiencies." → Kit 2 or Kit 3
- **Email list (non-supplement converters):** "Your results flagged low Vitamin D. Here's what that means for your energy." → Daily Stack

**Budget Week 4:** £900/month retargeting only. No cold Meta yet.

### Week 6: Supplement Pre-Order Gate

Before committing £3,500–8,000 to supplement inventory, run a pre-order mechanism:

**Mechanism:**
- Kit buyers whose results recommend supplements receive an email:
  > "Based on your results, we're recommending the Daily Stack. We're taking pre-orders now at a 10% discount (£31.46/month for first 3 months). Reserve yours."
- Small deposit (£5–10) secures the pre-order, applied to first month's subscription

**Gate:** If 25+ pre-orders are received, place the supplement MOQ order immediately.

### Week 6+: Cold Meta at Scale

Only activate cold Meta ads once:
- You have 3+ genuine influencer testimonials/posts (social proof)
- Supplement pre-orders are confirmed
- Website has real reviews or results data published
- Retargeting campaigns have 2 weeks of optimisation data

**Cold Meta creative strategy:**
- **Hook (first 3 seconds):** Symptom statement or pattern interrupt. "If you're 42 and you're knackered by 3pm every day, this video is for you."
- **Body (4–25 seconds):** Problem + data-led solution.
- **CTA (final 5 seconds):** "Test yours from home. £44. Results in 72 hours."
- **Format:** Vertical video (9:16) for feed and stories. Minimum 3 creative variants at launch.

**Meta targeting parameters (cold):**
- Age: 35–58
- Gender: Male
- Location: UK
- Interests: Men's health, health and wellness, physical fitness, gym, running, biohacking, nutrition
- Exclude: Existing customers, current website visitors (last 30 days)

## Supplement Conversion Funnel (Post Kit Result)

Once a kit result is delivered, a timed email sequence activates. This is the supplement conversion engine.

### Email Sequence: Kit 2 Result → Supplement Subscription

| Email | Timing | Subject | Content |
|-------|--------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Result delivery | "Your results are ready" | Dashboard link + summary of findings |
| 2 | +1 day | "What your [low Vitamin D / elevated CRP] result means" | Explanation in plain language. No jargon. |
| 3 | +3 days | "Here's exactly what we recommend — and why" | Personalised supplement recommendation with EFSA claims. Pre-order CTA. |
| 4 | +7 days | "Quick question about your results" | Short, personal check-in. Soft CTA. |
| 5 | +14 days | "Most men who tested with [X deficiency] noticed this by Week 6" | Outcome framing. Last direct supplement CTA. |
| 6 | +28 days | "Time to retest?" | Kit 3 upsell |

> Email 3 is the highest-converting touchpoint in the funnel. The personalised recommendation ("your CRP is elevated — this is why we recommend collagen, specifically") is the product differentiator that no competitor can replicate.

## CQC Parallel Track

The CQC application runs independently and simultaneously with Phase 0 — it is not gated on supplement profitability.

**CQC trigger:** 40+ founding member deposits received.

**Why 40 deposits?**
- Proves genuine demand for regulated clinical services
- Each deposit represents a known future patient at £185/month
- Provides cash float (40 × £75 = £3,000) to offset CQC application costs
- De-risks the 3–6 month wait period — you have a known patient intake on approval day

**CQC preparation begins alongside Phase 0 (regardless of deposit count):**
- Clinical governance documentation (Dr Ewa Lindo to lead)
- DSP pharmacy selection (Pharmacierge or Healistic)
- E-prescribing system selection (SignatureRx or Healistic)
- CQC Registered Manager appointment
- CQC application submission target: Month 3 of Phase 0

## KPIs and Tripwires

### Weekly KPIs (Phase 0)

| Metric | Target | Tripwire |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Kit sales (weekly) | 15–25 | < 5/week for 2 consecutive weeks = review acquisition |
| Blended CAC (paid channels) | ≤ £50 | > £55 sustained for 2+ weeks = pause and rebuild |
| Google Search CTR | > 5% | < 2% = rewrite ad copy |
| Supplement conversion rate | ≥ 15% of kit buyers | < 10% after 80 results = restructure email sequence |
| Founding member deposits | 5–10/month | Review deposit CTA if < 3/month |
| Affiliate kit sales | > 30% of total sales | If < 15%, outreach more aggressively |

### Monthly KPIs (Phase 0)

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 | Month 6 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Cumulative kit sales | 40–70 | 130–200 | 300–500 |
| Supplement subscribers | 0 | 15–30 | 50–100 |
| Supplement MRR | £0 | £465–930 | £1,550–3,100 |
| Founding member deposits | 5–15 | 25–45 | 50–80 |
| Affiliate active count | 10–20 | 25–50 | 40–70 |

### Expansion Decision Framework

**Expand supplements (add SKUs) when:**
- Supplement MRR > £2,500/month
- Churn rate tracking ≤ 3%/month
- 2+ customer requests for a specific product type

**Expand kits (add panels) when:**
- Kit-to-supplement conversion > 22%
- Kit volume > 100/month consistently
- A biomarker gap is identified from customer feedback

**Trigger CQC push when:**
- 40+ founding member deposits received (regardless of supplement performance)
- Application should already be in progress from Month 3
