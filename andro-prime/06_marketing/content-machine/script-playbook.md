# Short-Form Script Playbook (Spine B founder content)

**Owner:** Keith Antony | **Status:** Craft reference v1, 2026-07-09 | **Read first:** `avatar-mark.md`, `hook-playbook.md`, `founder-content-system.md`

The repeatable craft for turning a topic into a finished **short-form** script (Reel / Short / TikTok), fast, in Andro Prime's voice and inside the compliance rails. The hook is only the open; this doc owns the body. Adapted from a studied four-step scriptwriting system and gated hard by compliance. For long-form (YouTube), the title/thumbnail and a 20-to-30-second intro are a different craft: see `content/youtube-founder-journey-strategy.md`.

Every script is written to **Mark** (`avatar-mark.md`), in the **Peer / Storyteller** voice, and runs `/compliance-preflight` before it ships.

---

## The four blockers, in order

A script gets stuck on four questions. Answer them in this order and the blank page goes away.

1. **Interestingness.** What about this is actually worth watching?
2. **Hook.** How do I stop the scroll? (Owned by `hook-playbook.md`.)
3. **Story.** How do I hold them after the hook?
4. **Engagement.** What makes them like or share at the end?

---

## Step 1. Interestingness: compliant shock-facts

Interest comes from **shock**: the gap between what the viewer already believes and what you tell them. Bigger gap, more pull. Before writing, jot three to five facts with the biggest true gap for Mark.

**Where our shock legitimately comes from** (not from claims):

- **Myth-busts.** "Men don't get iron deficient" is believed and wrong. Highest-shock, fully compliant.
- **Real biomarker facts.** What ferritin / B12 / hs-CRP actually is and does, stated plainly.
- **The system gap.** "Normal" means average, not healthy. The markers a basic GP panel skips.
- **Verbatim customer truth.** The pain lines in `avatar-mark.md` §C are pre-validated shock ("blaming my exhaustion on bad sleep for a year").

**The hard gate** (this is where the borrowed system needs a rail): a shock-fact must be **true AND compliant**, never just shocking. A high-shock line that is a claim is a breach. Specifically:

- No cure / treat / diagnose / fix, and no "this test fixed my fatigue."
- No fabricated numbers. If a fact leans on a figure, it must be Keith's real result or a cited third party; otherwise flag it.
- Stay inside EFSA wording for any supplement mention; ashwagandha never appears.
- Do not infer low testosterone from Kit 2 markers (compliance red-flag table).

If the only way to make a topic "shocking" is to break a rail, pick a different fact. There is always a compliant one.

---

## Step 2. Hook

Pick the hook using `hook-playbook.md` (the six archetypes, visual-first, dream-outcome-is-certainty, founder-reveal-held). Use `/hook <topic>` to generate three, then choose one for the script.

Three extra opening angles from the studied system, folded into our archetypes:

- **Comparison** ("normal vs optimal", "what a basic panel checks vs what actually matters"). Pairs with Contrarian.
- **List** ("3 markers your GP skips"). Pairs with the Listicle story structure below.
- **Problem** ("if you're tired every afternoon..."). Powerful but **do not agitate to sell**. State the problem, do not twist it. Education side only.

---

## Step 3. Story structure (the body)

Pick one structure, write its outline on the page, then slot the shock-facts in order. Four fit Andro Prime short-form. Each line in the finished script is spoken words plus a bracketed [visual] and [text] cue.

**A. Personal story (our home).** Keith's own journey. Founder-as-feature.
`Hook (relatable tired man) -> the story (start: how I felt / middle: what I did / end: what I found) -> the founder turn (so I built this) -> soft CTA`
Pairs with: Personal-experience / Experimenter hook. The founder reveal lives in the "end/turn", never the hook.

**B. Problem-solver.** Lead with the felt problem.
`Hook (problem) -> name the problem plainly (no knife-twisting) -> the shift (test, don't guess) -> the answer it gives -> soft CTA`
Pairs with: Problem or Question hook. Compliance care on the agitation step.

**C. Breakdown.** Explain one thing simply.
`Hook -> quick context/shock -> what it is (e.g. what ferritin actually measures) -> why it matters to a tired man -> soft CTA`
Pairs with: Secret-reveal / Investigator or Contrarian hook.

**D. Listicle.** An ordered set.
`Hook (list) -> item 1 -> item 2 -> item 3 -> soft CTA`
Pairs with: List hook. Each item stands alone (e.g. three markers a basic panel skips).

Keep the body tight: short-form is 30 to 60 seconds. Layer facts in the order a mate would need to follow them, add small transitions, cut every word that is not load-bearing.

---

## Step 4. Engagement: one target emotion

Pick **one** emotion before writing and use it as a filter on every line. Sharing happens when the viewer wants to transfer that feeling to someone else.

**Our safe, on-brand emotions:**

- **Surprise / curiosity** (primary). Myth-busts, "the number they skip". Safest and most shareable.
- **Recognition / relief.** "Someone finally gets it." The validation Mark has never had. Deeply on-brand.

**Handle with care:**

- **Vindication / mild anger.** "Told you were fine, and you're not." Works, but aim it at the reference range and the system, never the GP or the profession. Sparingly.

**Avoid:**

- **Fear.** Do not drive health anxiety to sell. ASA vulnerability risk, and off-brand.

Write the chosen emotion at the top of the script as a reminder, then gut-check the finished draft against it.

---

## The six-step write (Andro Prime version)

1. Mine three to five **compliant** shock-facts for the topic (Step 1). Flag any needing a real number.
2. Write the **target emotion** at the top (Step 4).
3. Pick and write the **hook** (via `/hook`, all four hook commandments: aligned, fast, clear, curious).
4. Pick the **story structure**, write its outline, slot the facts in order (Step 3).
5. Write the script: spoken lines plus [visual] and [text] cues, transitions, nothing wasted.
6. Read it back through the emotion filter and the compliance gate. Fix or bin.

---

## The four-check (before it leaves the page)

1. Is this genuinely interesting to Mark?
2. Is it as compressed as it can be?
3. Does the hook hook on its own?
4. What one emotion do I feel at the end, and is it the one I aimed for?

Then the non-negotiable fifth: **does every line pass compliance?** Run `/compliance-preflight`. No cure claims, real numbers only, ashwagandha silent, no low-T inference from Kit 2, every claim inside available markers, founder disclosed in the video.
