# SOP — LinkedIn Post (Keith's founder halo)

**A LinkedIn post from a pillar or a founder moment, in Keith's voice, on his personal profile.** LinkedIn is the founder halo and the one-way feeder into the brand (`youtube-founder-journey-strategy.md` §2); it is not a brand channel and the audiences never merge.

**Trigger:** a calendar slot (~2-3 / week) or a founder moment worth a post.

**Roles:** agent drafts; Keith edits to his voice and posts; Ewa only for a net-new claim.

---

## Steps

1. **Pick the angle** from a signed pillar or a genuine founder observation. Feeling-first; maps to a live-kit marker if it references one.
2. **Draft to `templates/linkedin-post.md`:** one strong hook on line 1, the diagnostic-question arc, ends on a question to the reader. **No bullet lists** (`02_brand/CONTEXT.md`). 300-500 words, fragments at pivots (`tone-of-voice.md` §6).
3. **No engagement-bait.** No "comment YES / agree," no pods, no automation. LinkedIn rewards substance and dwell time and demotes bait.
4. **Run `/compliance-preflight`.** Same rails as any external copy. Net-new claim → Ewa.
5. **CTA is soft.** Brand halo, newsletter, or a link to the pillar. Keep the value on-platform; do not link-dump. Personal → brand only.
6. **Keith posts.** In his voice, his profile. Agent does not auto-post.

---

## Definition of done

- Hook on line 1, ends on a question, no bullet lists, in Keith's voice.
- Passed pre-flight; no bait; soft CTA.
- Posted by Keith.

## Anti-checklist (stop if any is true)

- The post uses a bullet list or an engagement-bait CTA.
- It reuses a verbatim opener already used elsewhere (AI tell).
- It asserts a claim beyond the signed canonical asset.
