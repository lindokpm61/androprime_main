## 1. The non-negotiables that shape every founder asset

These come first because they override any tactic below.

- **Feeling-first.** Open on the body-feeling a man actually searches ("recovery takes three days now," "wired but tired," "flat by 2pm"), never the marker. The biomarker is the answer, not the hook (`tone-of-voice.md` §7, feeling-first doctrine).
- **Marker must map to a live kit.** Every hook maps to a marker in a currently-available kit: testosterone (Kit 1/3), or Vitamin D / Active B12 / ferritin / hs-CRP (Kit 2/3). Do not promote cortisol, thyroid, or metabolic testing until those kits launch (`social-channel-setup.md` Content source).
- **No new claims.** Derivatives inherit the canonical blog asset's Ewa sign-off and may not exceed it. A net-new claim goes back to the canonical asset and to Ewa.
- **Founder testimonial limits.** Keith may show his own data and journey. He may **not** assert a supplement caused a change. Show the numbers and the tracking; let the viewer draw the line. On retests: "how my levels have changed," never "what fixed them."
- **The hard rails, every time:** no diagnose / treat / cure / fix; no "TRT is available" (use "be first when we launch"); EFSA-approved supplement wording only, and the Daily Stack's silent formulation ingredient is never named or shown on a label (silent-ingredient rule, `03_compliance/CONTEXT.md`); "Ewa-approved recommendation logic," never "reviewed by our doctor"; no em dashes; CTAs go to Kit 1/2/3 or the email rung, never the founding-member list.
- **Andropause / male-menopause is Ewa-gated.** The strongest founder hook is also the highest ASA risk. Do not produce an andropause-framed episode until the Pillar E pack is Ewa-signed (`03_compliance/claims-and-labels/pillar-E-andropause-claims-pack.md`).

## 2. Formats (the shapes Keith records)

All in Keith's diagnostic-observer voice, feeling-first. Match the density in `tone-of-voice.md` §6 (video short: 100-150 words, most lines 3-7 words, a fragment every other line).

- **Standard yap (talking to camera).** Phone on a tripod, lav mic, window light. Keith to camera, one take where possible. The base unit. Gear floor is already set in `youtube-founder-journey-strategy.md` §6: do not let a gear upgrade delay recording.
- **Dashboard / screen-record.** Keith walking through his own numbers on the results dashboard using the Ewa-approved recommendation logic (not a bespoke interpretation). This is the founder-journey read-through.
- **Graphic-overlay yap.** Talking-to-camera with the number, the range, or the marker name shown on screen as text or a simple card (roughly one visual per couple of seconds keeps attention), built to the brand thumbnail/visual rules. Good for the muted-autoplay feed.
- **Walking / car yap.** Candid, in motion. Use sparingly; it adds variables. Keep it for genuinely candid founder moments, not the core explainer.

Record once, cut many: every long-form YouTube explainer is the source for 30-60s vertical pulls (Shorts / Reels / TikTok) and a native Facebook post (`youtube-founder-journey-strategy.md` §6). See `sops/sop-atomise-pillar.md` and `sops/sop-founder-short-form.md`.

## 3. The founder series (episodic, because episodic earns return visits)

The platform data confirms episodic content earns return visits, and Andro already has the arc: the founder-journey line in `youtube-founder-journey-strategy.md` §5 (Ep 0 baseline → Ep 1 what the numbers said → Ep 2+ retest checkpoints). This doc packages that arc, plus the marker explainers, into a **feed-native, named short-form series** so a viewer knows what is coming and comes back.

**Two series lines, both compliant by construction:**

1. **The founder-journey series** (peer trust): Keith's own baseline and retests over time, feeling-first, real numbers, "how my levels changed" framing. This is the single most persuasive and most compliant asset the brand can make. Film the baseline before results move; the before-state is unrecoverable.
2. **The marker / normal-range series** (authority): one repeatable episodic hook that reframes what "normal" hides, atomised from the blog pillars. Each episode: a feeling → the number → what the range actually measures → the next step (including "that is a GP conversation").

**Series name: "Read Your Blood" (locked by Keith, 2026-07-09).** Plain, on-brand, scales to any marker. Considered and rejected: *"The Normal-Range Lie"* (strong POV but the "lie" framing carries avoidable pre-flight risk) and *"One Number at a Time"*.

"Read Your Blood" is the on-screen series tag and opening beat, kept identical across episodes so the audience recognises it. Keep the *device* consistent and the *wording inside each episode* fresh (the no-repeated-openers rule in `tone-of-voice.md` §3 applies to founder scripts too).

## 4. Hook and story structure (imported craft, compliance-safe)

Use the storytelling structure from the research, translated to the diagnostic-observer voice. The four-move Keith arc (`tone-of-voice.md` §2) already maps cleanly onto it:

| Story beat (research) | Keith move (voice) | Example (compliant) |
|---|---|---|
| Stakes / open on a concrete moment | Move 1: concrete moment, not a claim | "A bloke my age brought me his GP results. Testosterone 12. 'Normal,' they said." |
| The question / open a loop | Move 2: the pointed diagnostic question | "So I asked the one thing his GP hadn't. What's the range?" |
| The head-fake / the reframe | Move 4: "It's not X. It's Y." | "It's not a normal-range problem. It's a question-mismatch problem." |
| Leave them wanting more | Move 5: close with a question to the reader | "What number did your GP actually give you?" |

- **Hook in the first frame, visually.** About half of feed video is watched muted, so the hook must land in on-screen text and image, not just the audio.
- **Earn the share, do not bait it.** A genuine "send this to a mate who's always knackered" beats "comment YES." Engagement-bait CTAs are demoted on Facebook and LinkedIn.
- **No hook-swap off other people's clips.** Originality is enforced on every platform now and it is already an Andro rule. Keith's own footage, own numbers, own voice.

Ready-to-use, pre-flight-clean hooks live in `templates/hook-bank.md`, drawn from the feeling keyword set (`seo-ai-search/tools/staging-feeling-first/`) and `content/track-a-launch-copy.md`.

## 5. Per-platform rules (corrected for platform reality)

Same voice and same claims everywhere; different shape, cadence, and CTA per platform.

### Instagram (`@keith.androprime`, Creator account)
- 30-60s Reels, feeling-first, visual hook in frame one. Vertical 9:16.
- Bio link to the feeling-first landing page / test-selector, UTM-tagged (`social-channel-setup.md`).
- **Comment-to-DM (ManyChat).** A user comments a keyword ("tired," "brain fog") and the tool auto-DMs a link to the test-selector / funnel. This is the primary Instagram conversion mechanism alongside the bio link. It is compliance-gated: keywords map to live-kit feelings only, the DM copy runs pre-flight, the link never routes to the FM list, and ManyChat must be added as a data sub-processor before launch. See `sops/sop-comment-to-dm.md` and `templates/dm-keyword-map.md`.
- Sends and saves are the discovery proxies; earn them, do not bait them.
- Sustainable ~1-3 Reels / week. Consistency over volume.

### YouTube (`@keithandroprime`, "Keith Antony · Andro Prime," Brand Account)
- Two lines, both per `youtube-founder-journey-strategy.md`: 8-12 min explainers (intent + authority) and the 6-10 min founder journey (peer trust).
- Length-agnostic; optimise for retention and satisfaction, not a length target.
- Custom thumbnail on every video (`sops/sop-thumbnail.md`). Description router to the best live kit. Attribute the clinical review to the **article the video is atomised from** ("Based on our article X, clinically reviewed by Dr Ewa Lindo, GMC #4758565"), never a bare "clinically reviewed" on a video Ewa has not seen (`templates/youtube-description.md`, corrected 2026-07-09).
- Shorts are the 30-60s pulls from the long-form.

### Facebook (brand page, older segment)
- **Native uploads, not lazy Instagram cross-posts.** Cross-posting a Reel can strip licensed audio or misroute it to Feed; upload native and use Facebook's own audio library.
- Original informational framing (Meta demotes compilation / reaction content). No engagement-bait CTAs.
- In-post link to the router, but the post must stand on its own value; Meta is testing link-post caps for unverified pages, so do not lean on link-dumping.
- ~2-3 / week, older-segment feeling-first education.

### LinkedIn (Keith's personal profile, the founder halo and feeder)
- Native text and short native video. One strong hook on line 1, ends on a question, **no bullet lists** (`02_brand/CONTEXT.md`). Keith's voice only.
- Substance and dwell time win; virality does not. No engagement pods, no "comment to agree," no automation.
- One-way cross-promotion: personal → brand. The audiences never merge.
- ~2-3 / week.

### TikTok (optional, low priority for this ICP)
- Authenticity is officially endorsed there, and the same vertical cut works. But this ICP skews older, so treat TikTok as opportunistic reuse, not a core channel.

### Reddit (not a posting channel)
- Keith participates authentically on r/UKTRT, r/testosterone, r/malehealth. Never pitch, never link unprompted (`02_brand/CONTEXT.md`). This is trust-building, not distribution; it is out of the atomisation flow.

## 6. What this system deliberately does not do

- **No unsolicited mass-DMing.** That is a spam-flag and a compliance risk. (User-initiated **comment-to-DM** via ManyChat, where the user comments first, IS adopted as an Instagram conversion mechanism, compliance-gated: `sops/sop-comment-to-dm.md`. Email / Customer.io stays the deeper capture and nurture layer.)
- **No unverified guru numbers.** Ignore "sends worth 3-5x likes," "two ideal YouTube lengths," and "7-11-4 as a ranking rule." They are not platform-confirmed (`platforms-vs-gurus-2026.md`).
- **No reposting or hook-swapping other people's content.** Penalised by the platforms and banned by Andro.
- **No posting faster than Keith can sustain.** The founder is the bottleneck; protect his time and never go silent. Batch-record (2-3 explainers per session) to stay ahead.
