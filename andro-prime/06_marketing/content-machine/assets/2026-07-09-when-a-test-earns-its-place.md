## Script

> Three weeks of honest changes. Same wake time every day. Last coffee before two. Two drink-free nights.
>
> Better. Not right.
>
> So what now?
>
> This is where a blood test earns its place, and not before.
>
> Four things worth seeing when tiredness won't shift. Vitamin D. Active B12. Ferritin, which is your iron stores.
>
> And hs-CRP: a protein your liver releases when your immune system is irritated.
>
> It isn't a diagnosis on its own. It won't do the work the basics do.
>
> What it gives you is a baseline instead of a hunch. And a clearer conversation if you take it to your GP.
>
> We don't diagnose. We don't prescribe. We measure the markers, explain the numbers, and tell you the next step, including when that step is your GP.
>
> Education, not medical advice.
>
> What have you actually changed, and for how long?
