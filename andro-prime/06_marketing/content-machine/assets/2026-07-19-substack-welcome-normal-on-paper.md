I'm Keith Antony, founder of Andro Prime.

For years I did the right things and still felt flat. Tired by mid-afternoon, slower to recover, foggy in a way I could not explain. Every time I raised it, the answer was the same: your bloods are normal.

Normal. While I felt anything but.

So I stopped taking "normal" as the end of the conversation and started actually measuring: the markers most men never get tested, read properly, in plain English. That is why Andro Prime exists, and it is why I am writing this now. I am doing it in the open, my own numbers included, as I go.

### What to expect here

This is the plain-English version of what your blood can actually tell you. Expect:

- What "normal" really means on a blood test, and why normal is not the same as optimal
- The markers people rarely check when they feel tired, flat, and slow to recover: things like vitamin D, B12, ferritin, and inflammation
- What the numbers mean, without the hype or the magic-supplement nonsense
- My own results as I test and retest myself

Roughly one post a week. No spam, no fear-mongering, no miracle cures.

This is education, not medical advice. Always speak to your GP about your health.
