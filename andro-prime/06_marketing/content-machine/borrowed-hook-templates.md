# Borrowed Template Bank (from "1000 Viral Hooks")

Curated for Andro Prime founder content (Spine B). This is a RELEVANCE + COMPLIANCE filter of the 1000 Viral Hooks library, not a quality ranking of the originals. Every template here has been cleaned, re-slotted to our placeholder style, mapped to one of our six archetypes (Fortune Teller, Experimenter, Teacher, Magician, Investigator, Contrarian), tagged to a funnel stage, and given one compliant Andro Prime example.

**House rules baked into every example below:**

- Dream outcome is CERTAINTY (the answer), never a cure, fix, or symptom relief.
- Blood is the key visual. Lead with the tired man; hold the founder reveal back.
- Real numbers only. Any figure appears as `[your real X number]` until the baseline shoot exists.
- Only these markers: ferritin, vitamin D, B12, hs-CRP, testosterone. No thyroid/cortisol/metabolic implied as something we test.
- No TRT promise or implication. No andropause/libido angle (Pillar E, unsigned). Ashwagandha never named.
- Contrarian energy points at the reference range or the system, never at the GP as a person.
- Placeholders used: `(marker)`, `(symptom)`, `(number)`, `(pain point)`, `(time frame)`, `(age)`, `(action)`, `(the answer)`.

Every line still runs `/compliance-preflight` before it ships. Nothing here is pre-approved.

## Relevance ranking (verdict per category, ranked)

1. **MYTH BUSTING (gold, richest per line).** The smallest category but the highest hit-rate for us. Our whole wedge ("normal is not optimal", "men do get iron deficient", "it is not just your age") is a myth-bust, and this category is built for exactly that contrast. Almost every keeper is a Contrarian aimed cleanly at the reference range or a false belief, which is our most powerful and most dangerous archetype. Highest yield.
2. **COMPARISON (gold, visual-native).** Small but perfectly suited: side-by-side is how you show two "normal" results that feel completely different, or a number before you knew versus the number you were walking around with. Blood-and-number visuals slot straight in. Thin in volume, high in fit.
3. **EDUCATIONAL (high, deep bench).** By far the largest category and a deep well of "here is the one marker most tired men never check" Teacher/Investigator structures. Lower hit-rate than Myth Busting (a lot of money/DIY/food filler), but the sheer volume means plenty survive. Our workhorse for MOFU explainers.
4. **STORYTELLING (high, but only the founder-journey lane).** Two distinct halves. The "X years ago I was exhausted and blaming my sleep" / "I did everything right and still felt flat" / "I was shocked when I found out" lane is pure Andro Prime gold and maps straight onto Mark and Keith. The other half ("started a business", "flipping a house", "day # of my project") is noise for us. Cherry-pick hard.
5. **DAY IN THE LIFE (medium, documentarian).** Useful for the founder Ep 0 baseline and the retest checkpoints (documentarian voice), but most entries are generic "day in the life of a (title)" filler. A few good series-opener and retest-marker shells.
6. **RANDOM (cherry-pick, thin).** Mostly off-niche trend/comedy hooks. Two or three Contrarian belief-statements are worth lifting; the rest is skip.
7. **AUTHORITY (mostly avoid).** This is a trap for us. We are Peer, not Authority (per the hook playbook). The category is built on guru voice: "my client got X result", "I became X at age Y", "my transformation ought to be studied". Almost all of it front-loads an outcome claim or an authority posture we cannot use. Kept only 2, both hard-reframed to peer/founder/experimenter voice with the outcome stripped out.

## MYTH BUSTING (kept 7 of ~26)

**Verdict:** Gold. Our wedge lives here. Keep the Contrarian lines, aim every one at the range or a false belief, never at the doctor.

1. **Template:** No, your (symptom) is not just (common wrong cause).
   - Archetype: Contrarian | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "No, your exhaustion is not just your age."
   - Note: aim at the belief ("it's just age"), not at the GP who said it.

2. **Template:** More men need to hear this: (common belief) does not mean (the real thing).
   - Archetype: Contrarian | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "More men need to hear this: a 'normal' blood result does not mean an optimal one."
   - Note: this is the core wedge. Keep it on the reference range, never "your GP is wrong".

3. **Template:** Being (symptom / state) is not just about (the obvious marker).
   - Archetype: Investigator | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "Feeling flat is not just about your testosterone. For a lot of tired men it is a number they never think to check."
   - Note: this one earns its place by steering off the TRT gravity toward ferritin/D/B12. Do not let the follow-up imply a low-T diagnosis.

4. **Template:** Let me de-influence you from (faith-based action).
   - Archetype: Contrarian | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "Let me de-influence you from buying another supplement before you have actually tested what is low."
   - Note: fine to say "test first"; do not tell anyone to stop a prescribed medication.

5. **Template:** This is why doing (blind action) keeps you (stuck state).
   - Archetype: Teacher | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "This is why stacking supplements blind keeps you guessing instead of knowing."
   - Note: frame the outcome as "guessing vs knowing", never "supplements do not work".

6. **Template:** They said "(the reassuring line)." The (marker) said otherwise.
   - Archetype: Contrarian | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "They said 'you're normal, it's probably just stress.' My ferritin said [your real X number]."
   - Note: point the contradiction at the phrase and the range, not at the person. Real number only.

7. **Template:** You are not (bad label). You are not (bad label). You just have never seen (your number).
   - Archetype: Contrarian | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "You're not lazy. You're not past it. You might just have never seen your own iron number."
   - Note: "never checked / never seen the number" is compliant. Do not assert the viewer is deficient (no diagnosis).

## COMPARISON (kept 4 of ~20)

**Verdict:** Gold for the visual. Best home for the two-results-that-both-say-normal shot and the before-you-knew number.

8. **Template:** Both these results are the same on paper. I have not changed a thing. But this man feels (state) and this man feels (state).
   - Archetype: Contrarian (visual) | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "Two men, same age, both told their bloods are 'normal'. One feels great, one feels flat. Here is the number the reference range hides."
   - Note: strongest visual in the whole bank for the normal-vs-optimal wedge. Keep it about the range, not the GP.

9. **Template:** This is (marker) before I knew. This is the number I was walking around with.
   - Archetype: Magician (cold open on the number) | Stage: TOFU
   - Example: "This is my ferritin the year I felt flat. This is the actual number: [your real X number]."
   - Note: frame as "before I knew", not "before and after". No implication a supplement moved it.

10. **Template:** Cheap (thing) versus expensive (thing): which one actually (delivers the answer).
    - Archetype: Investigator | Stage: BOFU
    - Example: "A finger-prick blood test versus a £200 private consultation: which one actually tells you your numbers."
    - Note: comparison/BOFU. Keep claims to what the kit measures; no diagnostic or clinical-service implication.

11. **Template:** This is what your (marker) looks like when you (do the responsible thing), and this is what it looks like when you (guess).
    - Archetype: Magician (visual) | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "This is what it looks like to actually know your vitamin D. This is what it looks like to guess at it all winter."

## EDUCATIONAL (kept 15 of the largest category)

**Verdict:** High. Deep bench of Teacher/Investigator shells. Our MOFU workhorse. Skip the money/DIY/food filler.

12. **Template:** If I woke up (pain point) again tomorrow and wanted (the answer) by (time frame), here is exactly what I would do.
    - Archetype: Investigator | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "If I woke up exhausted again tomorrow and wanted to know why by the weekend, here is the first blood marker I would check."
    - Note: the dream is the answer/certainty, not "fix my energy".

13. **Template:** If you are (age range) do not (blame the wrong thing) before you have checked (marker).
    - Archetype: Teacher | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "If you are a man over 35, do not write your tiredness off as age before you have checked your ferritin."
    - Note: "checked" is a measurement, not a treatment. Do not promise the check changes how they feel.

14. **Template:** Everyone tells you to (generic advice) but nobody tells you (the specific how). Here is the (# second) version you can save.
    - Archetype: Teacher | Stage: MOFU
    - Example: "Everyone tells you to 'get your bloods done' but nobody tells you which numbers matter. Here is the 60-second version, save it."
    - Note: MOFU explainer, routes to the email rung, not a kit.

15. **Template:** What happens to (marker / symptom) when you go (time frame) without (the thing).
    - Archetype: Investigator | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "What happens to your energy when your vitamin D quietly sits low all winter."
    - Note: mechanism education only. Do not promise correcting it resolves the symptom.

16. **Template:** Things that are quietly (draining a state) without you realising it.
    - Archetype: Teacher | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "Things quietly draining your energy that you would never spot without a blood test."
    - Note: lands the "people adjust to feeling bad" pain. Keep it to what a test can reveal.

17. **Template:** If you have (symptom), (symptom) and (symptom), you might be (guessing) instead of (measuring).
    - Archetype: Investigator | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "If you are tired, foggy and cold all the time, you might be guessing at the cause instead of measuring it."
    - Note: this is the compliant cousin of the banned "if you have X you probably have Y" diagnosis line. Stop at "measure it", never name a condition.

18. **Template:** You are not (label). You just need to (see the number). Here is how.
    - Archetype: Teacher | Stage: TOFU/MOFU
    - Example: "You are not just lazy or getting old. You might just have never checked your iron, and here is how you would."
    - Note: "see / check the number" only. No diagnosis, no promised outcome.

19. **Template:** This is what my (marker) looked like when I felt (fine), and this is what it looked like when I felt (flat).
    - Archetype: Magician (visual) | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "This is what my bloods looked like the years I felt fine. This is the one that looked different the year I felt flat: [your real X number]."
    - Note: real numbers only, both frames. The number is the story, not any intervention.

20. **Template:** In 60 seconds I am going to teach you more about (your marker) than (the rushed system) has time for.
    - Archetype: Teacher | Stage: MOFU
    - Example: "In 60 seconds I will teach you more about your own testosterone number than a 10-minute appointment has time to."
    - Note: aim the jab at the time pressure of the system, not at the clinician. Keep it light.

21. **Template:** It took me (# years) to learn this, I will give it to you in under a minute.
    - Archetype: Teacher | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "It took me years of guessing to learn this. I will give it to you in under a minute: check the number before you buy the supplement."

22. **Template:** Why did it take me until (age) to realise (the simple truth).
    - Archetype: Teacher (peer) | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "Why did it take me until 44 to realise one finger-prick could answer what years of guessing never did."
    - Note: dream is the answer. Founder reveal is the turn, not the opener.

23. **Template:** I spent (# years) with (the wrong approach) so you do not have to.
    - Archetype: Teacher / Experimenter | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "I spent two years supplementing everything under the sun except the one thing I actually needed to check."
    - Note: near-verbatim of the avatar's strongest pain line. "check", not "the one thing that fixed me".

24. **Template:** The reason you cannot (get the answer you want) is (a number no one has shown you).
    - Archetype: Investigator | Stage: TOFU
    - Example: "The reason your energy keeps crashing might be a number nobody has ever put in front of you."

25. **Template:** Stop (faith action) if you actually want to (know).
    - Archetype: Contrarian | Stage: TOFU

    - Note: "know" is the outcome. Do not tell anyone to stop a prescribed treatment.

26. **Template:** Did you know these four things are the ones most (audience) never check.

## Excluded on compliance grounds (representative, not exhaustive)

These structures were dropped or can only survive a full reframe. Main failure modes:

- **Cure / heal / fix / reverse claims.** "I haven't done (action) in # years and it healed my (noun)" ("healed" is banned; only usable if reframed to "found out / measured", never healed). "(marker) can be reversed, here are # ways to improve it." "I don't know who needs this but if you have (symptom) this is how I (dream result) every single time" (guarantee + implied cure).
- **Diagnosis from symptoms.** "If you have (symptom) you probably have (condition)." "Give me 10 seconds and I'll tell you if you have (X) or (Y)." We stop at "check the number", we never name the condition.
- **Supplement-efficacy before/after.** "This is what your (noun) looks like when you take (supplement) versus when you don't." "Swap these for better (result)." "If you take (noun) it will (result)."
- **EFSA / clinically-proven traps.** "(ingredient) is better for (result) than (ingredient), and I'll back it with studies." "(ingredient) is actually really good for (result)." We only use the approved EFSA wording, and never for a silent ingredient.
- **Cure-promise phrasing.** "Is it possible to stop (pain point) and (dream result) in (time frame)." "There is absolutely no reason for you to be (pain point) every day."
- **Authority-voice outcome claims (most of the AUTHORITY category).** "My client got (dream result) without (pain point)." "I became (achievement) at (age)." "My transformation ought to be studied." Wrong posture (we are Peer) and they front-load a result we cannot promise.

**Tally:** roughly a dozen individual templates were dropped outright, plus the large majority of the AUTHORITY category (~28 of 30) was set aside for the peer-voice conflict and implied outcome claims. The dominant reasons, in order: cure/heal/reverse/fix language, diagnosis-from-symptom, supplement before/after efficacy, and Authority-voice guarantees.
