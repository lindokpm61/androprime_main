# Hook Bank (Ewa-safe, feeling-first)

Ready-to-use opening hooks per pillar. Every hook here is **feeling-first** (opens on the body-feeling, the marker is the answer), maps to a **live-kit marker**, and is written to pass `/compliance-preflight`. They are hooks, not claims: a hook may name a feeling or a question; it may not diagnose, promise, or assert causation. Draw from these and rephrase (no repeated verbatim openers across assets, per `tone-of-voice.md` §3).

Source language: the feeling keyword set (`seo-ai-search/tools/staging-feeling-first/`) and `content/track-a-launch-copy.md`. Compliance law: `03_compliance/CONTEXT.md`.

> **Do not** use andropause / male-menopause / libido hooks until Ewa signs the Pillar E pack. Do not use cortisol / thyroid / metabolic hooks until those kits are live.

## Rules for using a hook

- Rephrase so no two assets share the same opener wording.
- Keep the marker as the answer, never the hook.
- Add the disclaimer where the format allows: "Education, not medical advice."
- Route the CTA to the live kit or the email rung, never the founding-member list.
- Run the finished asset through `sops/sop-compliance-route.md`.
