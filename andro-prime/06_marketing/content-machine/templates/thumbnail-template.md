# Template — Thumbnail (brand-locked design spec)

Docs-only spec for the three thumbnail surfaces. Produced by hand in Figma / Canva (no code). Visual law: `02_brand/visual-identity.md`. Visual north star: the satori OG card at `09_website-app/frontend/app/api/og/blog/[slug]/route.tsx`. Procedure: `sops/sop-thumbnail.md`.

---

## The three surfaces

| Surface | Size | Use |
|---|---|---|
| YouTube long-form | 1280 x 720 (16:9) | Every YouTube video. |
| Shorts / Reels / TikTok cover | 1080 x 1920 (9:16) | First-frame hook for vertical video. |
| Facebook / LinkedIn share | 1200 x 630 | Link posts pointing at a pillar. |

Blog OG card is generated by the satori route and left as-is (not produced here).

---

## Brand-locked rules (from `visual-identity.md`, non-negotiable)

- **Ground:** white `#FFFFFF`. **Type:** black `#000000`, Inter `font-black` (900), uppercase, `tracking-tighter` for the headline.
- **No accent colour, no gradient, no glow, no shadow, `rounded-none`** (square corners), square linecaps.
- **Logo:** the refined monogram (`components/shared/Logo.tsx` / `02_brand/assets/logos/refined-monogram/`). Small and consistent; do not restyle.
- **Photography:** if used, a real man 38-55, rendered **grayscale** (stock is in the brand DON'T column). **No stock, no fitness models, no lifestyle gloss.**
- **Structure over decoration:** structural black borders, not effects. Inverted variant (white on black) allowed where it reads better.

---

## Text rules

- A few words only, legible at feed size and when muted. The **feeling is the hook; the marker is the answer** (do not put the clinical term as the headline).
- Plain English. **No em dashes.** UK English.
- **Mini pre-flight on the text** (`sops/sop-compliance-route.md`): no diagnose / treat / cure; no "TRT available"; no supplement-efficacy (EFSA-approved wording only; the silent ingredient is never named); no FM-list framing; no claim the canonical asset does not make.
