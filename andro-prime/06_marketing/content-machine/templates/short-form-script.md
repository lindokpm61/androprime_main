# Template — Short-Form Script (Reel / Short / TikTok)

30-60s vertical, Keith's voice, feeling-first, 100-150 words, fragment-dense (`tone-of-voice.md` §6). Hook must work muted (on-screen text). Run through `sops/sop-compliance-route.md` **before recording**.

---

## Fill-in structure

- **Pillar / marker:** (must map to a live kit)
- **Canonical asset it inherits from:** (the signed blog pillar)
- **On-screen hook (frame 1, muted-legible):** ______
- **Series tag:** "Read Your Blood" (locked; `founder-content-system.md` §3)
- **Voiceover (four-move arc):**
  1. Moment / feeling (concrete, not abstract): ______
  2. The pointed question / the test: ______
  3. The reframe ("it is not X, it is Y") + the compliance hedge (signal, not cause; GP not supplement): ______
  4. Question to the viewer + disclaimer: ______
- **Caption:** feeling-first, UTM bio link, no engagement-bait.
- **CTA target:** Kit ___ / email rung (never FM list).

---

## Worked example (Pillar G — inflammation / hs-CRP → Kit 2)

**On-screen hook:** "Recovery used to take a day. Now it takes three."
**Series tag:** Read Your Blood.

**Voiceover:**
> The session you'd shake off by morning now hangs around for three days. Same sleep. Same training. The body just isn't clearing it.
>
> So I tested mine.
>
> One of the markers was hs-CRP. High-sensitivity C-reactive protein. It's a protein your liver releases when your immune system is irritated.
>
> Mine was up.
>
> That doesn't tell you why. It tells you there's a signal. The why is a conversation with your GP, not a supplement order.
>
> A tired week feels like a training problem. Sometimes it's a signal you've never measured.
>
> What did your last blood test actually check?
>
> Education, not medical advice. Find out what your blood says. Link below.

**Caption:** "Three-day recovery isn't just age. I measured mine. What did your last test check? Education, not medical advice. Test link in bio."
**CTA target:** Kit 2 (carries hs-CRP) via the router.

*Why this passes: feeling-first; shows Keith's own data without asserting a supplement caused anything; hedges signal-not-cause and routes to the GP; no diagnose/treat/cure; EFSA-approved wording only (silent ingredient never named); marker is live-kit; no FM CTA; no em dashes.*
