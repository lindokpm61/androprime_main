
> **Attribution rule (corrected 2026-07-09).** Do **not** write a bare "Clinically reviewed by Dr Ewa Lindo" on a video she has not reviewed. Under the hybrid role split she signs off the canonical article and the recommendation logic; she reviews a founder script only when it carries a net-new claim. A claim-free derivative video therefore never reaches her, and the bare line would assert a review that did not happen. Attribute the review to the **article** the video is atomised from, as above. If Ewa *did* review the script (any net-new claim, or an Ewa digital-twin short), the bare line is accurate and may be used. Authority: `content/youtube-founder-journey-strategy.md` §8.
