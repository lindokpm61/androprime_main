Turn the man who Googled "thyroid test" — because he's tired/flat and has read thyroid might be why, or a panel
flagged his TSH — into a **waitlist signup** (and, on launch, a Kit 5 buyer) by giving him the most useful UK

## 7. Opening block (AI-snippet target)

> **Direction:** A thyroid test (often a "thyroid function test") is a blood test that checks how your thyroid

## 9. Section-by-section content brief

- **What a thyroid test measures** — TSH first (counter-intuitive: high TSH = underactive thyroid; explain the
  feedback loop plainly), then Free T4/T3, then TPO antibodies (autoimmune marker, Hashimoto's). Concrete opener.
- **Why thyroid gets missed in men** — the differentiator section. Thyroid content + awareness is female-framed;
  men attribute the symptoms (fatigue, weight, low mood, cold) to age/stress/training. This is the
  time-on-page + citation hook no incumbent writes.
- **What counts as "normal" — the UK ranges** — TSH/T4/T3 reference ranges, each sourced; note lab variation +
  the "normal band vs your optimum" point (link to Pillar C `myth-of-normal-range`). The subclinical
  (TSH high-normal, T4 normal) nuance — flagged carefully, route to GP, never diagnose.
- **Symptoms worth taking seriously** — list hypo + hyper symptoms as *things worth testing for*, never "you
  have an underactive thyroid". Frame as "if several of these persist, a test is reasonable."
- **Borderline/abnormal result** — what high vs low TSH points toward, in plain English, always "may suggest"
  + GP. TPO-positive = autoimmune tendency, route to GP.
- **What a single result does NOT tell you** — defuse; TSH fluctuates (time of day, illness); one borderline
  number isn't a diagnosis; thyroid is read as a pattern over time.
- **Baseline → retest** — the loop in thyroid terms (retest at 6–12 weeks for borderline TSH, standard clinical
  practice). Link Pillar F when live.
- **When to see your GP, not us** — non-negotiable. Markedly abnormal TSH, symptoms + abnormal result,
  pregnancy/trying to conceive (thyroid matters), neck swelling/lump (urgent), palpitations + weight loss
  (possible hyper, urgent). Enabling framing.
- **How Andro Prime will measure this — waitlist** — thyroid panel on the roadmap (Kit 5); join the waitlist;
  no fake "buy now".
