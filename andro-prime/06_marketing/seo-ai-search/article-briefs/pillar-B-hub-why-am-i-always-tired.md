Help a tired 35–50yo man understand the **lifestyle** reasons he feels flat, give him things to change

## 9. Section-by-section content brief

- **Causes:** practical, evidence-aware but NO claims/thresholds. Sleep: consistency + quality > hours.
  Stress/cortisol-feel: keep behavioural, not biochemical. Training: the recovery-debt idea (reuse the
  inflammatory-markers lifestyle framing, lighter). Alcohol/caffeine/blood sugar: behavioural levers.
- **What to change this week:** 4–6 concrete actions (sleep window, caffeine cut-off, a deload/walk,
  alcohol trim, protein+fibre breakfast). This is the shareable core.
- **When it's more than lifestyle (boundary):** see your GP if tiredness is sudden/severe, with weight
  loss, breathlessness, low mood that won't lift, or persists despite weeks of genuine lifestyle change.
  This is the section Ewa sight-checks.
- **Where a check fits:** soft, honest — "if you've changed the inputs and still feel flat, a check can
  show whether something measurable (e.g. vitamin D, B12, iron) is contributing; that's a GP conversation,
  or a check like our Energy & Recovery Check." Mention markers only as "things a test looks at," never as
  "you have a deficiency." No thresholds.
