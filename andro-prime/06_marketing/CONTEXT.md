# Marketing Workspace Context

## Read before any copy work

Before writing kit pages, supplement pages, LP copy, or email copy, read:
- `../04_products/icp-kit-supplement-alignment-april2026.md` — defines the correct selling frame, hero claims, what each kit may and may not claim, ingredient copy hooks, and dashboard flow
- Root `CLAUDE.md` — brand voice, EFSA claims, compliance rules

Copy that conflicts with `icp-kit-supplement-alignment-april2026.md` must be revised. That document supersedes V7 product docs on all copy and UX decisions.

---

## Purpose
This workspace governs acquisition, content, channels, campaigns, and traffic strategy.

Use it for:
- campaign design
- affiliate plans
- paid media planning
- SEO and AI-search content strategy
- article briefs
- LP planning
- content planning
- attribution and analytics structure

## What belongs here
- marketing plan
- acquisition strategy
- LP strategy and LP briefs
- blog and GEO planning
- channel plans
- affiliate programme docs
- campaign assets and briefs

## Good output looks like
- channel-aware
- tied to a funnel stage
- matched to one audience and one offer where relevant
- clear about canonical vs LP page roles
- measurable

## Do not use this workspace for
- compliance signoff
- product threshold logic
- backend implementation details
- customer support or retention flow ownership unless the task is acquisition-linked
