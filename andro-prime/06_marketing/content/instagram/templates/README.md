## Brand assets used

- **Navy** `#1A2B3D` — primary background and chrome.
- **Oxblood accent** `#8C2A2F` — kicker text, accent rules, stat-card hero number.
- **Warm neutral** `#F4F1EC` — feed quote-card background.
- **Type:** DejaVu Sans (sub for Helvetica/Inter) for headings and body, DejaVu Serif Bold for the feed quote-card hero quote. Substitute with the brand's actual typeface in Canva — the templates are dimensionally identical.

## How to use these in Canva

1. **Upload each PNG to Canva** as a "background" element (Uploads → Drag the PNG into a 1080×1920 or 1080×1080 canvas).
2. **Place text as overlay layers** on top of the placeholder zones — the dark navy box in the middle of the Story templates is where your face/photo/clip goes; the kicker, headline, and body text are already typeset but you can either keep them as PNG or replace with editable Canva text layers.
3. **Replace `[YOUR CODE]`** with the PT's actual code (e.g. `PTMARK15`).
4. **Export** as PNG at 1× resolution. Stories at 1080×1920, feed at 1080×1080. No need to compress further — Instagram will re-encode.

## How to use directly (no Canva)

Each template is publishable as-is once `[YOUR CODE]` is replaced. The placeholder zone (the lighter rectangle in Story templates) is dark enough that you can drop a portrait photo into the area without compositing — just upload a face/kit photo to the same dimensions on top in any image editor.

For the Reel cover (`05-reel-cover-1080x1920.png`), this is the **first frame** of your Reel — the first 0.5 seconds. The rest of the Reel is your face delivering the script in `pt-programme.md` §5E. The cover frame is what shows in the IG feed grid before someone taps play; that's the only frame this template is for.

## Compliance reminders (read before posting)

- **Do not use Story 1 if you didn't receive a free kit.** It claims personal experience. Posting it without having tested the kit is a Section B violation per the Brief and triggers immediate code revocation.
- **The "10% off" copy is hard-coded into the chrome** — do not edit it to say 15%, 20%, or any other percentage. Per Section D of the Brief, exact 10% is the only permitted phrasing.
- **The vitamin D stat card (`07-feed-post-stat-1080x1080.png`)** uses the figure "40% of UK men over 40 are vitamin D deficient." This figure is widely cited but not from a single source — if anyone asks for the source, point to NICE / Public Health England vitamin D guidance for context. Do not present it as Andro Prime original research.
- **Every Reel and Story posted with your code requires a visible `#ad` or `#gifted` disclosure** if you received a free kit (influencers) or are earning commission. The templates do not include the hashtag — add it as a sticker in Stories or in the caption for Reels and feed posts.
