## 2. Brand architecture (where the channel lives)

**Decision (2026-06-08, naming amended 2026-07-09): the videos launch on a company-owned YouTube Brand Account, founder-branded, with Keith as the named founder and on-camera host.** Not a separate Keith-personal health channel, and explicitly not Keith's existing business-advisory personal brand (the one the voice samples come from).

**Why not the existing personal brand.** That brand's audience is founders/operators on scaling and process. Men searching "why does recovery take three days now" are a different audience. Putting testosterone/inflammation content there dilutes both followings and confuses the positioning.

**Why not a separate personal health channel.** The whole point of Engine B is that it *compounds*: search rank, subscribers, the AI-citation moat (Pillar F), the E-E-A-T. If that equity lives on a personal channel it is portable to Keith but is **not an asset of the business**. That matters for company valuation, for the CQC-registered entity, and for the day another presenter (or Ewa) needs to carry content. Building the moat on a personal channel is building it on land the company does not own.

**Why founder-fronted-on-a-brand-account.** Keith gets the authenticity (visibly the founder, testing his own blood, "bloke my age") without the equity leaking out of the company. The **account type** (Brand Account: transferable, manager-addable) is what keeps the moat a company asset; the **account name** is free to carry his face and his name. His face *powers* the brand. This is the root-CLAUDE.md framing ("personal brand is a product feature") applied literally.

**How it fits what's already decided:**
- The author/Person schema, `/authors/keith-antony/`, and the GP-reviewer attribution already point at andro-prime.com. The trust signal is "named founder + GMC GP, on the brand."
- GTM already splits the channels: **LinkedIn = Keith's personal founder halo** (B2B/press, the feeder); **blog + YouTube = the owned Andro Prime engine** (the home). So personal brand is the feeder, Andro Prime is the home.
- ASA complaints land on Andro Prime regardless, so the channel should be unambiguously the brand's owned, disclaimered, GP-reviewed surface.

**The model in one line:** the company owns the channel; Keith fronts and names it; his personal LinkedIn/founder presence drives traffic *into* it. Cross-promotion runs one way (personal → brand); the audiences never merge.

**Channel naming — RESOLVED 2026-07-09 (Keith): founder-branded.** Handle `@keithandroprime`, channel name `Keith Antony · Andro Prime`, created as a **Brand Account** so it stays transferable and company-owned. Ewa and any future presenter appear as guests on the founder's channel, which is the normal shape for a founder-fronted brand. Setup spec: [social-channel-setup.md](social-channel-setup.md). Decision record: [2026-07-09-youtube-channel-naming.md](2026-07-09-youtube-channel-naming.md).

> This supersedes the 2026-06-08 resolution recorded here (pure "Andro Prime," no co-brand on the name). That decision conflated account *name* with account *ownership*; the Brand Account type carries the ownership, so the name is free to carry the founder. The accounts shipped founder-branded on 2026-07-02.

## 4. Line 1 — Long-form explainers

**The format fit.** The older searcher researches before buying and does not convert off a feed (reframe §4.2). A 10-minute "what your GP and the big testing brands skip on [marker]" explainer, Keith-presented, is the platform + format + trust match.

**Topic source = the canonical blog assets, in calendar order.** Each published hub/spoke gets one explainer atomised from it. First five, mirroring the [content calendar](../seo-ai-search/content-calendar.md):

1. Inflammatory markers / CRP (Pillar G) — *first published asset, already live; first video.*
2. Low vitamin D symptoms (Pillar A hub)
3. CRP blood test explained (Pillar D hub) — the biggest underserved cluster
4. The myth of the "normal range" (Pillar C spoke) — strong authority/POV piece
5. 14 signs of vitamin D deficiency (Pillar A spoke)

**Rule:** the video may reshape and shorten the canonical asset but **may not make a claim the Ewa-signed blog asset does not already make** (atomisation model §3). The blog post is the substantiation record; the video inherits it.

**Hook discipline:** open on the symptom language men actually search ("always tired", "brain fog", "belly fat"), not the clinical term. Title and thumbnail in plain English; the marker name is the *answer*, not the hook.

## 5. Line 2 — Founder-journey series (the start-now item)

**Why it cannot wait.** The before-state is the hook. Keith filming his **baseline now** (his own Kit 1 + Kit 3 Plus, real numbers) is the single most persuasive *and* most compliant asset the brand can make: genuine, peer, founder using the product. Once results move, the baseline is unrecoverable. **Do not gate on being camera-ready** (GTM §7).

**The arc (real-time, not pre-scripted):**
- **Ep 0 — Baseline.** Why he's doing this, the symptoms, taking the kits on camera, the raw numbers. Film **this week**.
- **Ep 1 — What the numbers said.** Walking through his own results using the dashboard + the Ewa-approved recommendation logic (not a bespoke interpretation — see compliance rail below).
- **Ep 2+ — Retest checkpoints.** Tied to the retest timeline; "how my levels have changed" framing, never "what fixed them."

**Cadence:** baseline + first read now; checkpoints as real retests land. Quality of honesty over production polish.

## 6. Production model (lean, solo-founder-realistic)

- **Gear floor:** one camera/phone on a tripod, a lav mic, a quiet room with a window. Do not let a gear upgrade delay Ep 0.
- **Workflow:** long-form scripted from the blog asset (Line 1) vs lightly-planned talking-head + screen-record of the dashboard (Line 2). Editing outsourced or templated; founder time is the bottleneck, protect it.
- **Atomisation downstream:** every long-form video is the source for Shorts (30-60s pulls) and a Facebook informational post (older segment) per the atomisation map. Film once, cut many.
- **Batching:** record 2-3 Line-1 explainers in a session to stay ahead of the content calendar.

## 7. CTA / routing

- **Single central router.** All video descriptions point at one routing config (GTM §8), so redirecting to Kit 3 Plus / Liver when they launch is one change, not a per-video re-edit.
- **Intent-match to the best live product:** inflammation/CRP videos → **Kit 2 now** (carries hs-CRP), redirect to Kit 3 Plus when live; liver content → **email capture / waitlist now**, point to the Liver kit at launch. Never route liver intent to a hormone kit.
- **Cold viewers → the free email rung first**, not straight to checkout (Path B, GTM §9): symptom guide / quiz → email → nurture → kit.
- **Never route into the Founding-Member list** (CQC/ASA; `feedback_fm_list_not_in_content`). Video CTAs go to Kit 1/3/3 Plus + the email rung only.

## 8. Compliance rails (Guardrail #1 — read before any script ships)

Video, especially the founder showing **real blood numbers** and any **andropause** framing, is high-ASA-risk. Every script runs the `compliance-preflight` skill and reads `03_compliance/CONTEXT.md` before recording. Non-negotiable rails:

- **No medicinal claims.** No "diagnose / treat / cure / fix." Use "find out what your levels are" and, on retests, **"how my levels have changed"** — never "what fixed them" (causation claim, no RCT).
- **Founder testimonial limits.** Keith can show *his own* data and journey, but a testimonial may not state a claim the advertiser couldn't make directly. He may **not** assert the supplement caused a change. Show the numbers and the tracking; let the viewer draw the line.
- **Andropause / male-menopause = Pillar E = Ewa + ASA gated.** If the founder journey leans into the "male menopause" narrative (the highest-leverage hook, ~10-15k vol), it cannot ship until the Pillar E compliance pack is Ewa-signed (GTM §12, dependency §10).
- **Phase 0 boundary.** No "TRT is available." Use "be first when we launch." No implication a prescription or clinical service exists.
- **Kit scoping.** Kit 1 tests testosterone only — do not frame Kit 1 as explaining general fatigue/energy (that's Kit 2/3).
- **Ashwagandha silent ingredient.** When Keith shows his Daily Stack on camera, the KSM-66 is **never named, shown on label, or referenced.** A claim about it lands the ASA complaint on Andro Prime.
- **Recommendation logic, not bespoke interpretation.** Frame dashboard read-throughs as "the Ewa-approved recommendation logic," never "Dr Lindo wrote my personal report" (`feedback_no_bespoke_clinician_interpretation`).
- **Attribution everywhere, but attribute what is actually true (corrected 2026-07-09).** Ewa signs off the **canonical article** and the **recommendation logic**; under the hybrid role split she reviews a founder script only when it carries a net-new claim. So a claim-free derivative video is one she never sees, and "Clinically reviewed by Dr Ewa Lindo" on that video asserts a review that did not happen. Attribute the review to the thing she reviewed:
  - **Default (derivative video, no net-new claim):** "Based on our article [title], clinically reviewed by Dr Ewa Lindo, GMC #4758565, Andro Prime's medical lead." Link the article.
  - **Where Ewa did review the script** (any net-new claim, and every Ewa digital-twin short): "Clinically reviewed by Dr Ewa Lindo, GMC #4758565" is accurate and stays.
  - Person schema attribution is unaffected: she is the medical reviewer of the site's clinical content.
