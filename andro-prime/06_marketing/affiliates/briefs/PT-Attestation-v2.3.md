# ANDRO PRIME — PT Affiliate Compliance Attestation

**v2.3 · 2026 · Confidential**

By signing below, I confirm that I have read in full the Andro Prime PT Affiliate Partner Brief (**v2.3**) and that I understand and accept the rules below. I acknowledge that my Andro Prime affiliate code (`PT[NAME]15`) will not be issued or activated until this attestation is countersigned by Andro Prime.

**1. Forbidden language (Section B of the Brief).** I will not use the words "diagnose", "diagnosis", "treat", "treatment", or "cure" in any content, DM, story, post, voice note, or in-person client conversation about Andro Prime. I will not state or imply that TRT is currently available. I will not state "you have low testosterone" — I will use "your results indicate…". I will not claim Andro Prime supplements treat any disease. I will not claim Andro Prime cured any condition. I will not use "clinically proven" without a specific RCT citation.

**2. The silent-ingredient rule (Section C of the Brief).** I will only ever name, or make claims about, Daily Stack ingredients that appear on the approved-claims list in Section C of the Brief, using only the approved wording. I will not name, list, or make any claim about any Daily Stack ingredient that is not on that list — in any context, ever, including captions, stories, voice notes, DMs, podcasts, in-person client conversations, or comments. If asked what is in the Daily Stack, I will use the approved answer in Section C. I understand that a single breach of this rule is a severe violation and results in immediate permanent revocation of my affiliate code.

**3. The 10% discount language rule (Section D of the Brief).** I will describe the customer discount as exactly 10% — never any percentage above 10%, never as "biggest discount", "exclusive deal", "limited time", "half price", or "sale". I understand inflated discount claims are a Section B violation under ASA rules and are subject to strike-1 enforcement on first occurrence.

**4. Personal-experience claims (Section A of the Brief).** If I have not received a free kit and have not personally bought and tested an Andro Prime kit, I will not claim personal experience of the product in any content. I will use the credibility-led framing in Section A instead. I understand that posting personal-experience pitch language without having tested the kit is a severe violation and triggers immediate permanent revocation of my code.

**5. ASA disclosure.** Where I post about Andro Prime in a context where I have received a free kit or am earning commission on referrals, I will include #ad or #gifted as required by ASA rules. The disclosure will be visible without the viewer needing to expand the caption.

**6. Weekly audit and three-strikes enforcement (Section G of the Brief).** I understand that all content I publish in association with my affiliate code is subject to a weekly automated content audit plus periodic manual review, and the three-strikes policy: 1st flagged violation = written warning + post takedown within 48 hours; 2nd within 6 months = code suspended 30 days; 3rd within 12 months OR a severe violation = permanent code revocation.

**7. Kill-switch protocol.** I understand that if Andro Prime receives a regulator query, complaint, or journalist enquiry citing my content, my code will be paused within 4 hours, the offending post will be requested taken down, and an internal incident will be logged. I will cooperate with any post-mortem and response.

**8. Pre-clearance.** If I am unsure whether a phrasing crosses any of the lines above, I will send it to keith@andro-prime.com for pre-clearance before publishing. I understand pre-clearance is always faster and lower-risk than enforcement after the fact.

**9. Commission terms (Section E of the Brief).** I have read and accept the v2.3 payment terms in Section E: £15 flat fee per kit; 10% customer discount via my code; +£10 Kit 3 upsell bonus; +£10 supplement-conversion bonus where a code-attributed customer subscribes within 60 days. I understand and accept that:
- if a sale is refunded within 14 days, charged back, or a related subscription is cancelled within 30 days, the corresponding fee and/or bonus is voided and recouped from my next payout;
- payouts are monthly on the 15th for the prior month, subject to a £30 minimum threshold;
- Stripe transaction records are the source of truth for what is owed, and I have 14 days from each monthly statement to raise a dispute via affiliates@andro-prime.com;
- tier graduation bonuses (Silver £200 / Gold £400) are one-off, paid once per tier, in arrears, conditional on no active strikes and good standing, and may defer one month under the stated cash-floor contingency;
- I am responsible for my own tax on all payments received.

**10. Founding-member list.** I understand the Andro Prime founding-member list is a free, non-cash email opt-in. No payment is taken — the list is free and creates no right to any future service. Being on the list — whether as a customer or an affiliate — does not create any right to a future TRT or clinical service. I will not state or imply otherwise to clients.
