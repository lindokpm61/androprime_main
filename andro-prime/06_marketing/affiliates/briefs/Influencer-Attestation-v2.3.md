# ANDRO PRIME — Influencer Compliance Attestation

**v2.3 · 2026 · Confidential**

By signing below, I confirm I have read in full the Andro Prime Influencer Creator Brief (**v2.3**) and that I understand and accept the rules below. I acknowledge that my Andro Prime affiliate code (`[NAME]15`) will not be issued or activated until this attestation is countersigned by Andro Prime.

**1. Forbidden language (Section B).** I will not use "diagnose", "diagnosis", "treat", "treatment", or "cure" in any content, DM, story, post, voice note, or in-person conversation about Andro Prime. I will not state or imply TRT is currently available. I will not state "you have low testosterone" — I will use "your results indicate…". I will not claim Andro Prime supplements treat any disease or cured any condition. I will not use "clinically proven" without a specific RCT citation.

**2. The silent-ingredient rule (Section C).** I will only ever name, or make claims about, Daily Stack ingredients that appear on the approved-claims list in Section C of the Brief, using only the approved wording. I will not name, list, or make any claim about any Daily Stack ingredient not on that list — in any context, ever. If asked what is in the Daily Stack, I will use the approved answer in Section C. I understand a single breach is a severe violation and results in immediate permanent revocation of my code.

**3. The 10% discount language rule (Section D).** I will describe the customer discount as exactly 10% — never any percentage above 10%, never as "biggest discount", "exclusive deal", "limited time", "half price", or "sale". I understand inflated discount claims are a Section B violation subject to strike-1 enforcement on first occurrence.

**4. Personal-experience claims (Section A).** If I have not received a free kit and have not personally bought and tested an Andro Prime kit, I will not claim personal experience in any content. I will use the credibility-led framing in Section A. Posting personal-experience pitch language without having tested the kit is a severe violation = immediate permanent revocation.

**5. ASA disclosure (Section E).** I will include #ad or #gifted on every Andro Prime post — including Stories — visible without expanding the caption. I will not edit the disclosure away after publication. Posting promotional content without a visible disclosure, or editing it away after publication, is a severe violation = immediate permanent revocation.

**6. Authentic-experience requirement (Section E).** If I received a free kit, I will actually use the kit — including taking the test — before posting promotional content about it. Faking the experience is a severe violation = immediate permanent revocation. Honest neutral or critical reactions are explicitly permitted.

**7. Weekly audit and three-strikes enforcement (Section H).** I understand all content I publish in association with my code is subject to a weekly automated audit plus manual review, and the three-strikes policy: 1st = written warning + takedown within 48h; 2nd within 6 months = code suspended 30 days; 3rd within 12 months OR a severe violation = permanent revocation.

**8. Kill-switch & pre-clearance.** If Andro Prime receives a regulator query, complaint, or journalist enquiry citing my content, my code will be paused within 4 hours and the offending post requested taken down. If unsure whether a phrasing crosses any line, I will send it to keith@andro-prime.com for pre-clearance before publishing.

**9. Commission terms (Section F).** I have read and accept the v2.3 flat payment terms in Section F: £15 flat fee per kit; 10% customer discount via my code; +£10 Kit 3 upsell bonus; +£10 supplement-conversion bonus where a code-attributed customer subscribes within 60 days. I understand the influencer programme has no tier, graduation bonus, or contest.
