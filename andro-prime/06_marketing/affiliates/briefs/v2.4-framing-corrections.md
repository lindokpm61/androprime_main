# v2.4 Framing Corrections — GP-Report Wording

Proposed v2.4 wording corrections — requires Ewa re-approval; bundle with the pending CA-001/CA-002 solicitor pass. v2.3 files deliberately untouched to preserve the approval trail.

**Date raised:** 2026-07-07
**Owner:** Keith Lindo
**Reason:** Aligns partner-facing briefs to the canonical ruling in `03_compliance/CONTEXT.md` ("Ewa signs off the system, not individual reports" + Red-Flag table). "GP-built report" and "personalised report" imply bespoke per-patient clinical interpretation (a regulated-activity claim). The verb test (`02_brand/trust-signals.md` §2): past-tense authorship of the system is SAFE; a present-tense act on the customer is NOT. "Dr Ewa Lindo signs off all the report copy" is SAFE and is kept — only the "GP-built"/"personalised" chip changes.
**Proposed standard chip:** "GP-designed report" (authorship of the template, not the customer's instance). Ewa has final say on the customer-facing wording in her sign-off session.
**Status:** PROPOSED — not approved. Do not ship until Ewa re-approves and the CA-001/CA-002 solicitor pass lands.

### 2. Influencer-Brief-v2.3.md (line ~30, "Why Andro Prime exists")

**Current (verbatim):**
> A UKAS-accredited lab returns the result. Our GP, Dr Ewa Lindo, signs off the personalised report. The customer gets a plain-English answer and a specific recommendation based on their actual numbers — not a guess.
