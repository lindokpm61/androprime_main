# ANDRO PRIME — Gym Partnership Brief

**v2.3 · 2026**

## We test what the NHS won't.

Andro Prime is a UK at-home blood test for men 35–55. UKAS-accredited lab, GP-built report, plain-English recommendations. Three kits: Testosterone Check (£99), Energy & Recovery Check (£119), Hormone & Recovery Check (£179) — **from £99**. Founded by Keith Antony; Medical Director Dr Ewa Lindo, GMC-registered.

### What we offer your gym

- **£15 per referral** commission to each in-house PT, plus **£10 Kit 3 upsell bonus** and **£10 supplement-conversion bonus**.
- **10% member discount** via your gym's unique code (e.g. `GYM[NAME]15`).
- **Free Kit 2 (£63 RRP) for up to 3 of your head trainers** — first-wave gym-partner perk, capped at gyms onboarded by Week -4. (Other in-house PTs join on standard terms, no free kit.)
- **Co-branded LinkedIn post** from Keith featuring your gym, distributed to his founder audience at launch.
- **Pre-written gym-member newsletter paragraph** and a QR code for reception, linking to a gym-specific kit page.
- **FirstPromoter dashboard** so each PT can see their own referrals and commission.

### What we need from you

- Permission to email your in-house PT list directly with your introduction.
- Buy-in from one trainer or coach as the gym-side champion for the first 30 days.
- A QR code at reception linking to your gym-specific kit page (we generate it; you print it).
- A signed compliance attestation from each participating PT before their code is issued (we provide it; ~2 minutes via e-signature).
- Permission for your gym's name and logo on the "Trusted Partners" page on andro-prime.com (opt-in; revocable).

### What this is NOT

- **Not a fee.** We do not charge gyms or PTs to participate.
- **Not exclusive.** Your PTs can recommend other brands too — though most won't bother.
- **Not a clinical service.** Andro Prime is a wellness-tier blood test. Clinical TRT is in CQC application — **not active**.
