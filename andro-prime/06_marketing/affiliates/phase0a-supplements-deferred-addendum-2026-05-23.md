## 1. Plain-English statement

Between Phase 0a launch and Phase 0b (estimated 2 to 3 months), partner commissions apply to **kit purchases only**.

- The £15 base affiliate fee, the 10% customer discount, the £10 Kit 3 upsell bonus, the PT tier system, the one-off graduation bonuses, the quarterly + annual contests, and the flagship per-piece content payments **all operate exactly as written in each partner's v2.3 brief**.
- The **£10 supplement-conversion bonus does not pay during Phase 0a**, because supplements are not yet on sale and there is no subscription event to trigger it.
- The supplement-conversion bonus **activates automatically when supplements ship**, on the rates and rules already agreed in each partner's v2.3 brief. No re-attestation, no new sign-off, no re-onboarding.
- **Waitlist opt-ins from partner traffic do not pay commission** during Phase 0a or at any later point. The waitlist is an email opt-in, not a sale, and is treated the same as the Founding-Member non-cash list.

Nothing else changes. Vetting, brief content, attestation, compliance enforcement, three-strikes, kill switch, payout cadence, dispute window, refund/recoup mechanics, the silent-ingredient rule, and the Phase 0 / post-CQC boundary all remain exactly as approved.

## 3. How Phase 0a kit sales are handled at Phase 0b launch

When supplements ship and the supplement-conversion bonus activates, every kit sale recorded during Phase 0a is handled identically to a Phase 0b kit sale for bonus purposes. Specifically:

- The 60-day supplement-conversion window runs from the **original kit purchase date**, exactly as written in each partner's v2.3 brief.
- If a customer who used a partner's code in Phase 0a subscribes to a supplement within 60 days of their **kit** purchase (which may straddle Phase 0a and Phase 0b), the £10 supplement-conversion bonus pays in full on the partner's next monthly payout after the subscription clears.
- The 30-day cancel-recoup rule applies as written.
- If a partner's Phase 0a kit sale is more than 60 days old at Phase 0b launch, the window has expired and no supplement-conversion bonus pays on that sale. This is consistent with the v2.3 brief and not a change.

No back-pay, no retroactive top-up, no special Phase 0a credit. The bonus operates on the original terms from the first eligible sale onwards.
