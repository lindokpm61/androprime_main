## Retest loop — seq-04 (subscriber onboarding)

seq-04 must include a retest prompt at Day 75–80 (email 4), prompting the Day 90 kit retest:
- Offer: 20% off the relevant retest kit (subscriber-only discount)
- File to create: `email-sequences/seq-04-email-04-retest-prompt.md`
- Subject line options: "3 months in — time to check your numbers" / "Has your Vitamin D moved? Let's find out."
- Do not frame it as "find out if the supplement cured you." Frame it as "find out how your levels have changed."

Three outcomes are all wins: improved (confirm it's working), unchanged (investigate why — keeps engagement), worsened (surfaces founding member candidate, triggers Ewa conversation).
