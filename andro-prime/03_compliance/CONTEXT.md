# Compliance Workspace Context

## Purpose
This workspace governs wording, privacy, approvals, and risk boundaries.

Use it for:
- claims review
- privacy and governance
- deposit terms
- content approval flows
- red-flag language
- regulatory boundary checks

## What belongs here
- pre-launch compliance checklist
- privacy notices and policies
- deposit wording and terms
- claims-and-labels guidance
- content approval procedures
- data governance notes

## Good output looks like
- cautious
- specific
- operationally usable
- aligned with Phase 0 vs post-CQC boundaries
- written in a way other workspaces can rely on

## Do not use this workspace for
- general marketing ideation without a compliance angle
- product design unless the issue is regulatory
- UI design
- engineering implementation unless governance is the focus
