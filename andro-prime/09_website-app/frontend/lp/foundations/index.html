01
Marker 01 — Hormones

Total Testosterone

The total amount of testosterone in your blood. Your baseline. If this is low, everything else, energy, mood, drive, takes a hit.

02
Marker 02 — Hormones

SHBG

Sex Hormone Binding Globulin. It binds to testosterone and makes it unusable. High SHBG means your total T might look fine on paper while you still feel terrible.

03
Marker 03 — Hormones

Free Testosterone

The testosterone your body can actually use. Calculated from your Total T and SHBG. This is the number that matters most for how you feel day to day.

04
Marker 04 — Energy

Vitamin D

Most UK men are deficient, especially October to March. Low vitamin D directly affects muscle function, recovery, and energy. You won't know without testing.

05
Marker 05 — Recovery

Magnesium

The recovery mineral. Active men burn through it fast. Low magnesium means worse sleep, slower recovery, and fatigue that doesn't shift no matter what you do.

06
Marker 06 — Inflammation

hs-CRP

A high-sensitivity inflammation marker. Elevated CRP in active men usually points to joint and connective tissue stress. If your body's inflamed, it's not recovering properly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to fast before taking the test?
Yes. For the most accurate hormone baseline, you must take the sample fasted (water is fine) before 10 AM. Testosterone levels peak in the morning and decline throughout the day, and eating can suppress them temporarily.
How does the finger-prick work? Is it painful?
It's a very quick, small pinch. The kit includes medical-grade lancets that make a tiny puncture on the side of your fingertip. We provide detailed instructions and extra lancets to ensure you can collect enough blood easily at home.
How accurate is an at-home test vs going to a clinic?
The sample collection method is different, but the analysis is identical. Your sample is processed by the exact same UKAS ISO 15189 accredited laboratories used by private clinics and the NHS, using the same gold-standard testing equipment.
Are there any hidden subscription fees?
No. This is a one-off purchase of £69. You receive your kit, the lab analysis, and a digital report reviewed by a doctor. If you choose to follow any recommended supplement protocols later, those are separate, opt-in purchases with no obligation.
