The Data

Four markers. Four answers.

Each biomarker targets a specific reason your body isn't recovering the way it should. This isn't a general health screen. It's built for men who train.

01
Marker 01

Vitamin D

Most UK men are deficient between October and March. Low vitamin D directly affects muscle function, recovery speed, and energy. You can't tell from how you feel. You can only tell from your blood.

02
Marker 02

Active B12

The form of B12 your cells can actually use. Standard tests measure total B12 — which includes inactive fractions. Low Active B12 means fatigue, reduced mental sharpness, and slower recovery. More common after 40 than most men realise.

03
Marker 03 [Critical]

hs-CRP

A high-sensitivity inflammation marker. If this is elevated, your body is fighting something. In active men, it usually means joint and connective tissue stress that isn't resolving on its own.

04
Marker 04

Ferritin

Your iron stores. Low ferritin is one of the most common and most overlooked causes of fatigue in men. If your energy has dropped off a cliff, this is often why.
