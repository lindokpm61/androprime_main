Yes. Every ingredient is safe for healthy adults at these doses. But collagen products are most effective when you know you have confirmed inflammation. The blood test tells you whether this is the right product for you, or whether your joint issues have a different cause.

When will I notice a difference?

Most men report improved joint comfort within 4 to 6 weeks. Measurable changes in hs-CRP typically take 8 to 12 weeks. That's why we recommend retesting at 90 days.

Can I take this alongside the Daily Stack?

Yes. They're designed to work together. The Daily Stack covers your energy and recovery basics (zinc, magnesium, D3, B12). The Collagen focuses on connective tissue and joint comfort support. No ingredient overlap.

You can. But glucosamine doesn't address the collagen and vitamin C pathway, and it doesn't contain UC-II or MSM. This formulation covers joint support from multiple angles, not just one.

If your hs-CRP is above 10 mg/L, we won't recommend a supplement. That level of inflammation needs a GP. Your results report will say this clearly and provide a GP referral template.
