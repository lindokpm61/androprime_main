## 6. What We Stand For

**Eyebrow/Label:** OUR PRINCIPLES

**H2:** Four things we don't compromise on.

**Principle 1:** Data first.
Every recommendation we make starts with a result. Not a symptom questionnaire. Not a sales funnel. Your numbers, then your next step.

**Principle 2:** Plain English.
We don't write results reports that read like hospital discharge summaries. You shouldn't need a medical degree to understand your own health data.

**Principle 3:** Honest.
If your results are fine, we'll tell you they're fine. We don't manufacture urgency. We don't push supplements you don't need. The recommendation follows the data.

**Principle 4:** Building toward clinical.
We're a wellness platform today. We're working through CQC registration to offer medically supervised TRT in the future. The founding member programme is how we stay connected to the men who need it. When we're registered, they'll be first.
