The Data

We don't just check the surface.

The NHS usually tests one thing: Total Testosterone. That's only half the picture. We test the biomarkers that actually determine how you feel.

Total T

The total amount of testosterone currently in your blood. This is your baseline. If this is low, everything else suffers.

SHBG

Sex Hormone Binding Globulin. It acts like a sponge, soaking up your testosterone and making it unusable by your body. If SHBG is high, your testosterone might be normal but you'll still feel terrible.

Free T

The amount of testosterone that is actually available for your body to use. This is the number that drives your mood, drive, and focus.
