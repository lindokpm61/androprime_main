## 3. The Biomarkers (What You Get)
**Eyebrow/Label:** THE DATA
**H2:** We don't just check the surface.
**Subheadline:** The NHS usually tests one thing: Total Testosterone. That's only half the picture. We test the biomarkers that actually determine how you feel.

**Card 1:** Total T
**Description:** The total amount of testosterone currently in your blood. This is your baseline. If this is low, everything else suffers.

**Card 2:** SHBG
**Description:** Sex Hormone Binding Globulin. It acts like a sponge, soaking up your testosterone and making it unusable by your body. If SHBG is high, your testosterone might be normal but you'll still feel terrible.

**Card 3:** Free Testosterone
**Description:** The amount of testosterone that is actually available for your body to use. This is the number that drives your mood, drive, and focus.
