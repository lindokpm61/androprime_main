How to take your sample.

Takes about 5 minutes. Do this in the morning before 10am if possible. Testosterone levels are highest earlier in the day.

1

Wash your hands

Wash with warm water for 30 seconds to increase blood flow. Dry completely.

2

Use the lancet

Twist off the cap. Press it firmly against the side of your fingertip until you hear a click.

3

Fill the tube

Gently squeeze your finger. Wipe away the first drop, then fill the tube to the top line. Massage from palm to fingertip if slow.

4

Seal and pack

Snap the lid closed tightly. Place the tube in the biohazard bag, then into the original return box.

5

Post today

Use the pre-paid return envelope. Drop it in a priority postbox before the last collection of the day.

If you can't get enough blood, use the spare lancets provided on a different finger. Standing up and swinging your arm can help increase flow.
