# Strategy Workspace Context

## Purpose
This workspace holds the high-level business logic for Andro Prime.

Use it for:
- business model decisions
- sequencing and roadmap planning
- financial model framing
- scenario analysis
- strategic tradeoffs
- entity structure notes

## What belongs here
- master implementation blueprint
- roadmap documents
- financial model notes
- strategic decision memos
- market and competitive interpretation at business level

## Good output looks like
- structured
- decision-oriented
- explicit about assumptions
- realistic about constraints
- linked to operating phases

## Do not use this workspace for
- detailed page copy
- UI design
- code or CSS
- low-level execution checklists unless they directly support strategy
