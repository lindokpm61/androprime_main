# Decision: Andro Prime adopts the conflict-free positioning

**Date:** 2026-07-22 | **Decided by:** Keith | **Status:** ADOPTED. One dependency still owed: the exact customer-facing wording is locked with Ewa (see below) before the money-pages rewrite ships.

## The decision

Andro Prime's positioning is the **conflict-free men's health testing brand**: a men's-positioned testosterone/health testing company that is deliberately not a treatment funnel. Working articulation (internal): *"the men's testosterone test with no reason to sell you testosterone."* If a result is low, it routes to a GP and we earn nothing from that; we earn the same whether a customer's results are fine or not.

Origin: candidate position from the 2026-07-20 competitor teardown + Fable adversarial review (which killed the prior "own interpretation + tracking + trust" thesis — Thriva owns that triad). Confirmed against the mainstream buyer by the 2026-07-21 deep research (anti-upsell sentiment verified mainstream via Numan Trustpilot + ASA ruling A22-1153049, expressed as billing distrust).

**Scope (clarified 2026-07-22, Keith):** the position is NOT testosterone-only — the catalogue is three kits and all must conform. The testosterone articulation is the **spearhead** (press/GEO/comparison layer, where the market conflict is sharpest); the **governing principle is category-wide** and overrides the copy on every kit page: (1) any result that needs a doctor goes to a GP and earns us nothing (true on all kits: low T, low ferritin, every GP-tier route in the results engine); (2) no result changes what we offer or what it costs (substantiated by the locked Option 4 result-independent offer + the all-clear maintenance offer). Per-kit renderings: `02_brand/2026-07-22-conflict-free-wording-pack.md` §P and §D+. Note the precision trap this resolves: "a low result earns us nothing" is only universally true for GP-tier results; a low vitamin D on Kit 2 has a supplement offer nearby in Phase 0b, which is why the umbrella claim is the two-rule form, not the blanket "low result" form.

## Alternatives considered and why each lost (2026-07-22 review)

| Position | Evidence for | Why rejected |
|---|---|---|
| Speed/access ("know in days, no GP") | Strongest buyer evidence (52% buy speed; GP-bypass verbatim) | Not ownable — every competitor claims it. It stays the **hook** (sentence one), not the position |
| Reassurance ("tell me I'm fine") | Category centre of gravity (~93% no-escalation; Numan £128 "fear nothing") | Numan owns the wording at scale; no path to retest relationship or clinic. Absorbed via "bank it or money back" |
| Rigour ("test like a good doctor") | Two-sample confirmation, GMC GP, defensible cadence | Copyable (Medichecks owns accreditation). Becomes the **proof**, not the position |
| Men's Thriva (tracking/interpretation) | v1 thesis | Refuted 2026-07-20: incumbents own it; tracking is a self-selecting minority; reseller has weakest build hand |
| Symptom brand (brain fog etc.) | Demand is symptom-expressed | Acquisition strategy, not a position — already the content engine |
| Privacy ("we don't sell your data") | Live in product; Vitall data-landlord threat | Doesn't convert alone; honesty limits cap the claim. A **receipt** inside conflict-free |

Conflict-free is the only candidate that is structurally uncopyable (funnels can't follow without abandoning treatment revenue; generalists won't without abandoning a general-population audience), subsumes the others, and feeds the post-CQC clinic (the confirmed-low pipeline).

## Wording rails (binding on all copy)

1. **Never write "we will never sell testosterone."** The post-CQC clinic plans TRT; the absolute version detonates on clinic launch. The durable claim is **separation of incentives**: testing and treatment are separated; we earn the same whether the result is low or fine; treatment only ever follows an independent clinical decision. **The exact customer-facing sentence is Ewa+Keith's to lock** — owed at the batched Ewa sitting (ClickUp `869e7pmu9`), before the homepage/kits rewrite ships.
2. **Copy hierarchy (mainstream buyer):** speed/no-GP leads (earns the click), money honesty second ("one price, nothing hidden, we earn the same either way" — mainstream anti-upsell anger is billing distrust, not anti-TRT ideology). The sharp industry version ("no reason to sell you testosterone") is the press/GEO/comparison layer.
3. Existing honesty limits hold: never assert rivals sell data; "named GMC GP" is supporting texture, not the lead.

## What this unblocks

Money-pages rewrite (homepage, /kits, how-it-works — surfacing the live receipts: low-T→GP no-upsell, GP handoff, data controls, honesty note), Track A copy message-match, the B1 bundle pages (prices accepted 2026-07-21), GEO outreach framing.

## Falsifier (recorded so it gets tested, not forgotten)

If the quiz funnel-awareness question shows mainstream buyers don't perceive the TRT funnel, the fix is **emphasis, not repositioning** (speed/reassurance lead harder; position explains in sentence two). The structural moat is unaffected by buyer awareness of it.
