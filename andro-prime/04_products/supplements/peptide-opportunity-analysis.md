## 2. Opportunity A: Collagen Peptide Supplement (Phase 0 — No CQC Required)

### 2.1 Why This Fits Andro Prime

Collagen peptides are classified as food supplements under FSA regulation — no CQC, no GMC, no MHRA approval needed. They have genuine clinical evidence for joint health, skin elasticity, and connective tissue repair in men aged 35-60+ — your exact ICP. They naturally complement TRT (men on testosterone who are training hard need joint and connective tissue support).

The UK specifically is seeing rapid growth in collagen peptides within the sports nutrition and functional beverage sectors, with joint health becoming a higher priority for active men.

### 2.2 Product Concept: "Andro Prime Joint & Recovery Collagen"

**Positioning:** Not a generic beauty collagen. This is collagen for active men — targeting joint support, tendon/ligament health, and workout recovery.

**Formulation:**
- 10g hydrolysed bovine collagen peptides (Type I & III — joint, tendon, skin)
- 40mg UC-II undenatured Type II collagen (specific joint health claims, EFSA approved)
- 80mg Vitamin C (required cofactor for collagen synthesis, EFSA-approved health claim)
- 500mg MSM (joint comfort, anti-inflammatory)
- Optional: 5mg Hyaluronic acid (joint lubrication)

**Format:** Unflavoured powder (sachet or tub). Powder format dominates the collagen market at 58.4% share because it allows clinically relevant 10g doses — achieving the same via capsules requires 10-15 capsules per day.

**Regulatory position:** Food supplement. FSA regulated. EFSA-approved health claims only. Cannot make medicinal claims (e.g., "heals joints" or "cures arthritis"). Can make: "Vitamin C contributes to normal collagen formation for the normal function of cartilage."

## 3. Opportunity B: Prescription Peptide Therapy (Post-CQC)

### 3.1 What This Looks Like

Once CQC is approved and Ewa is prescribing TRT, adding peptide therapy as a premium add-on service for existing TRT patients. Most relevant peptides for the ICP:

**BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound):** Tissue repair, joint healing, gut health. Huge demand among active men 35-60 dealing with injuries, tendon pain, and slow recovery.

**Sermorelin / CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin:** Growth hormone secretagogues. Stimulate natural GH production for improved sleep, recovery, body composition, and anti-ageing.

**PT-141 (Bremelanotide):** Sexual function peptide. Acts on melanocortin receptors for libido and erectile function. Direct crossover with the TRT patient base.

### 3.2 Regulatory Framework for Prescription Peptides

These peptides are not MHRA-licensed for the uses described above. However, under GMC guidance, a doctor can prescribe unlicensed medicines ("specials") when:
- There is a specific clinical need
- No suitable licensed alternative exists
- The prescriber takes personal responsibility
- The patient gives informed consent

This is the framework The Catalyst Clinic, Menon Health, and other London peptide clinics operate under. It's legal but carries professional risk.

**Key compliance requirements:** GMP-grade pharmaceutical sourcing (not "research grade"), proper clinical assessment, informed consent documentation, adverse event reporting, and ongoing monitoring.

**Ewa's decision:** This requires Ewa to be comfortable prescribing off-label. It's a conversation for her, not something to assume.
