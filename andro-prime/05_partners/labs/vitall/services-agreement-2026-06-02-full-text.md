## PART A — CORE TERMS (apply to all Commercial Models)

**1. Background.** 1.4 The applicable Model in Part B is selected in the Order Form; only the selected Model's provisions apply. 1.5 Partner offers testing through its own customer journey; VITALL is a silent fulfilment, lab-coordination and reporting partner via the API Integration.

**2. Definitions (key ones).**
- **"Patient Transaction Value"** = gross amount paid by the Patient to VITALL for the Test, excluding shipping/rebooking fees unless agreed. (Note: under Model 3 Partner collects from the Patient, so this is mainly a definitional anchor.)
- **"Collection"** = collecting a Sample from a Patient and providing it to an Authorised Sub-Processor for analysis (by the sub-processor or by the Patient using a self-sampling kit).
- **"Commencement Date"** = date of last signature unless otherwise specified (Order Form specifies 08-05-2026).
- **"Services"** = provision of sampling kits, logistics coordination, phlebotomy, lab analysis via Authorised Sub-Processors, return of results and related services.

**3. The Services.**
- 3.1(b) Subject to delays outside VITALL's control, **Testing for most blood tests completed within 48 hours of receipt of a Sample and results provided within 24 hours thereafter.**
- 3.3 Collection services, upgraded devices, venous/nurse collection, **rebooking fees** and non-standard collection are NOT included in Test pricing and are charged separately.
- 3.4 No warranties except as expressly stated. 3.5 Simplified report formats; may omit some ISO 17025:2017 info (available on request). 3.6 Reports don't display the UKAS symbol; sub-processor labs are UKAS accredited.
- 3.9 Samples may be destroyed after Testing unless otherwise agreed. 3.10 **Results are informational only and do not constitute medical advice.** 3.11 Implied warranties (satisfactory quality, fitness) excluded to the fullest extent permitted.
- **3.12 If a Test fails completely or >50% of markers can't be reported, pricing includes a free repeat Test for failed markers only** (repeat determined by VITALL acting reasonably). 3.13 Repeat self-collection kits on request; repeated collection issues → may be encouraged to upgrade to venous.
- **3.14 Customer Relationship and Responsibility:**
  - 3.14.1 VITALL is responsible for performance of the Testing Services (kits, logistics, phlebotomy, lab analysis, reporting) via Authorised Sub-Processors.
  - **3.14.2 Partner = retailer/introducer/facilitator; does NOT provide lab analysis. Partner remains responsible for: (a) presentation/promotion/sale; (b) accuracy/legal compliance of descriptions & claims; (c) the customer purchase journey, including handling of refunds, cancellations and customer complaints not directly relating to the performance of the Testing Services.**
  - 3.14.3 Where Partner collects payment from the Patient, Partner is the contracting party for the sale; VITALL is fulfilment provider. VITALL responsible for obligations arising from performance of Testing; Partner responsible for obligations arising from sale/promotion.
  - 3.14.4 VITALL not responsible for: (a) inaccurate/misleading/non-compliant info from Partner; (b) Patient misuse; (c) failures from instructions/marketing not provided by VITALL.
  - 3.14.5 Mutual indemnity for breach / negligence / wilful misconduct (subject to clause 5 caps).
  - **3.14.6 Partner must reference VITALL as Testing Services provider in its T&Cs and on relevant product pages, including a link to https://vitall.co.uk/terms.**
  - 3.14.7 Patient-facing claims (tests, sample types, turnaround, results, clinical meaning, limitations) must be consistent with VITALL-approved/supplied wording.
  - 3.14.9 VITALL provides baseline approved wording; Partner may use it without further approval if materially unchanged. 3.14.10 VITALL responds to copy-approval requests within 5 Business Days; approval not given by silence; VITALL may reject non-compliant wording.

**4. Confidentiality.** Standard mutual; usual carve-outs (public domain, prior records, lawful third-party disclosure, legal requirement, professional advisers).

**5. Liability and Indemnity.**
- 5.2 No special/indirect/consequential loss (profits, savings, business/revenue, opportunity, goodwill, reputation, data).
- **5.3 Total aggregate liability cap = greater of £50,000 OR fees paid by Partner to VITALL in the preceding 12 months.**
- **5.4 Cap for Data Protection Losses / breach of confidentiality / breach of clause 6 = £100,000.**
- 5.5 Uncapped: death/personal injury by negligence; fraud; s.12 SoGA 1979 / s.2 SGSA 1982; anything not excludable by law.

**6. Data Protection.** 6.1 Roles per the Model (Order Form §6). 6.2 Controller–controller: each independently determines purposes/means, responsible for own compliance, handles its own DSARs, notifies the other of breaches affecting shared data. 6.3 Processor obligations (Art 28) where VITALL acts as processor; **6.3.4 Partner gives general written authorisation for Authorised Sub-Processors** (labs; kit/logistics/courier/postal; IT hosting; phlebotomy/nurse/clinic; advisers); VITALL notifies of material changes. 6.3.6 On termination, VITALL deletes/returns Personal Data at Partner's choice unless law requires retention. 6.4 No international transfers without UK-GDPR safeguards/derogation. 6.5 DP indemnity; caps unchanged (clause 5.4).

**7. Force Majeure.** Standard; if >1 month, either party may terminate (per 8.4 ref).

**8. Term and Termination.**
- 8.1 Initial Term **12 months** from Commencement (08-05-2026 → ~08-05-2027). 8.2 Auto-renews 12-month terms unless **≥90 days' written notice** before end of term (→ notice by ~07-02-2027 to exit at end of initial term). 8.3 Terminate for unremedied material breach (30 days). 8.4 Insolvency. 8.5 Force majeure >1 month.
- **8.7 Exit and Transition:** (a) VITALL continues to process **in-flight orders accepted before termination** unless agreed otherwise / unable; **(b) Partner remains liable for all fees, charges and costs incurred in respect of orders placed before termination;** (c) data return/delete/retain per DP law; (d) up to 30 days' transition assistance.

**9. General.** 9.5 Assignment needs consent. 9.9 Governing law England & Wales. **9.11 VITALL insurance at Commencement: PI (health data/reporting) £2,000,000; product liability £5,000,000; public liability £5,000,000; evidence on request.**

**10. Pricing Adjustments.** 10.1 VITALL may increase Schedule 1 prices where directly attributable to sub-processor/lab fee increases, law changes, or material logistics/consumables/compliance cost rises. 10.2 ≥30 days' notice. 10.4 Partner may terminate if it rejects an increase. 10.6 Renewal-term pricing review with ≥90 days' notice; reject → terminate at end of current term.

## PART B — COMMERCIAL MODELS · MODEL 3 — API / ELECTRONIC ORDER MODEL (the selected Model)

**1. Integration & Commercial Structure.** 1.1 Orders placed electronically via the API Integration. 1.2 Partner = customer-facing retailer and contracting party for the sale to Patients. 1.3 VITALL = fulfilment + lab-coordination provider via Authorised Sub-Processors. 1.5 Partner sets its own retail pricing.

**2. Payment.** 2.1 **Monthly invoicing in arrears.** 2.2 Payment within 14 days. 2.3 Interest 4% over base. 2.4 **Suspension for non-payment:** on ≥7 days' notice for undisputed overdue sums, VITALL may suspend placement of new orders, dispatch of kits, and/or release of results; not for bona-fide-disputed sums; resumes on payment.

**3. Cancellation (Model 3).**
- **3.1 Orders that have been processed by the laboratory are non-cancellable.**
- 3.2 Cancellation, repeat kit, upgraded device and rebooking fees apply as set out in Schedule 1.
- **3.3 Where Partner has charged the Patient directly, any Patient refund, retail price adjustment or customer compensation shall be Partner's responsibility unless otherwise agreed in writing.**
- 3.4 VITALL remains responsible for lab processing, sample logistics, result release and support relating to compromised samples / failed Tests / repeat Tests (per 3.12 + Schedule 1).

## SCHEDULE 1 — PRICING (Model 3)

**1. Commercial Structure.** 1.2 Prices = amounts payable by Partner to VITALL per Test/Package/Collection/kit/service ordered via the API. **1.3 Pricing applies per completed order and includes lab analysis, standard reporting and agreed result delivery unless otherwise stated.** 1.4 Prices are Partner cost prices; Partner sets retail.

**2. API and Order Mechanics.**
- **2.1 Orders are deemed placed when successfully transmitted via the API Integration and accepted by VITALL's systems.**
- **2.2 Orders that have been processed by the laboratory are non-cancellable.**
- **2.3 Cancellation / repeat-kit / upgraded-device / rebooking fees:**
  - (a) Orders processed by the laboratory are non-cancellable.
  - (b) Replacement / additional finger-prick / home sampling kit: **£12 per kit.**
  - (c) Replacement / additional Tasso / TAPII device: **£42 per kit.**
  - (d) Clinic rebooking fee: **£35** per rebooking.
  - (e) Nurse rebooking fee: **£45** per rebooking.
  - (f) **Any refund / retail price adjustment / customer compensation offered by Partner to a Patient is Partner's responsibility unless otherwise agreed in writing.**
  - *(NB: there is NO explicit fee for cancelling an order placed via the API BEFORE the laboratory has processed it. The only stated "non-cancellable" trigger is lab processing; pre-processing cancellation has no stated penalty.)*

**3. Collection Services.** Chargeable in addition unless expressly included; standard home-kit logistics included in Test price; upgrades/replacements/venous/clinic/nurse/rebooking charged separately.

**4. VAT.** Prices inc. VAT at zero rate for health services unless otherwise stated; non-zero-rated kits/items at applicable rate. **4.4 Prices are all-in per completed Test order, inc. VAT where applicable.**
