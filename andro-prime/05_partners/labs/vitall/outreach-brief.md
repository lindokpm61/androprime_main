## What to ask (informed by Thriva meeting)

The Thriva meeting surfaced several specifics that you now need to benchmark. Ask Vitall the same questions so you can compare like for like.

### Pricing
- What is the all-in cost for a capillary testosterone panel (Total T, SHBG, Free T calculated, Albumin, Free Androgen Index)? Break down: kit assembly, postage, lab analysis, results delivery.
- What is the cost difference between fingerprick and any alternative collection device (e.g. Tasso)?
- What is the incremental cost of adding individual biomarkers to an existing panel (e.g. adding Ferritin, Vitamin D, hs-CRP, Active B12)?
- Is there volume-based pricing? What does the per-test cost look like at 50/month, 200/month, 500/month?
- Do you offer clinical governance / doctor reporting for partners without CQC registration? If so, what does it cost per test? Does the fee drop when we obtain CQC?

### Minimum volumes
- Is there a minimum monthly volume commitment? If so, what is it and when does it kick in?
- What happens if we don't hit the minimum in the early months?

### API and integration
- Do you have a B2B API for order placement and results retrieval?
- Is there a sandbox or test environment?
- Is the API self-service or does it require your engineering team's involvement?
- Is API access included or is it a separate fee?
- What's the typical integration timeline?

### Biomarker availability
- Which biomarkers are validated for capillary postal testing?
- Specifically: is magnesium viable for postal, or do you have the same stability constraints as other labs?
- Is Active B12 available on capillary panels?
- Is hs-CRP available on capillary panels?
- Do you have a panel builder or biomarker catalogue we can review?

### White-labelling
- Can we fully brand the kit packaging, results interface, and customer communications?
- Does the customer interact with Vitall at any point, or is it entirely behind our brand?

### Contract terms
- What's the minimum contract length?
- Is there an exclusivity requirement?
- What are the notice/termination terms?

### Turnaround
- What is the typical turnaround from sample receipt to results delivery?
- Is there an SLA?
