Branding the Vitall platform (portal, results interface) with Andro Prime logos and colours is included at no cost, within their standard design framework. There is no setup fee and no monthly licence for this.

- **Branded physical kit sleeves** — cost not yet quoted; for future consideration when Andro Prime wants its own branded packaging on the physical kit

No platform fee. No API fee. No integration fee. The £58.50 / £63.00 / £98.00 per-kit prices (lancet) are the entire cost base.
