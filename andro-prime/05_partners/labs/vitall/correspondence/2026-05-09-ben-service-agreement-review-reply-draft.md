Hi Ben,

Thanks for sending the agreement through. I've been through it carefully and I'm keen to sign. There are just a handful of points I'd like to clarify or adjust first — most are straightforward, a couple are commercial.

1. Liability cap (Clause 5.3)

The £10,000 annual aggregate cap feels low for a diagnostics partnership, particularly given the indemnity in 3.14.5 and the data-protection indemnity in 6.5.1 are both subject to it. A single Personal Data Breach affecting our customer list could comfortably exceed that. Can we lift the cap — for example to the greater of £1m or 12 months of fees paid? Happy to discuss what's workable on your side.

2. Sub-processor list (Clause 6.3.4)

We're authorising VITALL to engage "Authorised Sub-Processors listed in the Agreement," but I can't see a list attached. Could you send the current list as a schedule, with a short notification clause when you add or replace one? Standard Article 28 territory.

3. Data Controller / Processor allocation

Clause 6.1.1 says the allocation is determined by the Order Form, but the Order Form doesn't state it. Under Model 3, where Andro Prime contracts with the patient and VITALL fulfils, my read is: Andro Prime is Controller for the customer relationship, VITALL is Processor for testing data. Can we add that explicitly to the Order Form so it's not ambiguous?

4. Schedule 1 — drafting gaps

Three things in Schedule 1 look unfinished:

- § 2.3 reads "Cancellation and rebooking fees apply as set out in Part B, Model 1, clause", with the clause number missing, and Model 1 isn't included in our agreement (we've selected Model 3). Could you supply the actual cancellation and rebooking fee schedule?
- § 3.2 references collection pricing "set out below" but no collection pricing table appears.
- § 4: could you confirm in writing the all-in VAT-inclusive price per kit? Health services are zero-rated but the kit components may not be, and I'd like the per-unit cost to be unambiguous before we model retail.

5. Insurance

Could you confirm VITALL maintains professional indemnity and product liability insurance, and at what level? Useful for our own compliance file.

6. Customer-facing copy approval (Clause 3.14.7)

Happy to keep our patient-facing claims aligned with VITALL's approved wording. To make this practical at our pace, could you (a) supply a baseline approved copy pack we can use as-is for turnaround times, sample types, and result limitations, and (b) commit to a five-business-day turnaround on copy approval requests? Otherwise our launch dependencies stack up on your inbox.

7. Exit / transition

The agreement is silent on what happens at termination — handling of in-flight orders, customer data return, and transition assistance. Could we add a short clause covering this?

Separately, still chasing the live API credentials and kit shortCodes so we can run end-to-end integration tests (per your earlier "don't fulfil orders" approach during testing). We're holding final QA on those.

Once the points above are settled I can sign and we move. Happy to hop on a quick call if it's faster than email for any of them.
