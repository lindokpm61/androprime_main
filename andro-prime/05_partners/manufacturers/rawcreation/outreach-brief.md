**Compliance note for label + claim text:** all customer-facing claims must stay inside the EFSA authorised-claims list. For these two products that means: Zinc "contributes to the maintenance of normal testosterone levels"; Vitamin D "contributes to normal muscle function"; B12 "contributes to normal energy-yielding metabolism / normal psychological function"; Vitamin C "contributes to normal collagen formation for the normal function of cartilage." Every other ingredient (including ashwagandha, MSM, HA, UC-II, glucosamine, chondroitin) is label-only with **no** health claim. Please do not add ingredient benefit claims to any label or product text without our sign-off.
