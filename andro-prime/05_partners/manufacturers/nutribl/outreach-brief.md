## Why Nutribl

- **"No MOQ" positioning** — claims attractive to startup volumes (needs verification for custom formulations specifically)
- **120+ stock formulas** — possible alternative path: start with closest stock formula match, customise later when volumes scale
- **Specific collagen private-label range** — useful for our Joint & Recovery Collagen, possibly reducing custom formulation effort and cost
- **Dropshipping option** — would meaningfully reduce launch fulfilment complexity (no inventory storage burden, especially while Gate 0A volumes are uncertain)
- **GMP certified** — meets baseline regulatory requirement
- **UK-based** — geographic alignment

## What We Want From This Conversation

1. **MOQ verification** — does the "no MOQ" claim hold for **custom** formulations, or only stock formulas?
2. **Stock formula option** — is there a Daily Stack-equivalent in their 120+ catalogue we could trial first?
3. **Their collagen private-label range** — does the existing formulation match our spec or how close can we get?
4. **Dropshipping economics** — per-unit cost differential vs traditional bulk-then-ship model
5. **3-tier pricing quote:** 1,500 / 5,000 / 25,000 unit volumes for each product
6. **Active B12 dose flexibility** — quote both 1,000mcg and 5,000mcg Methylcobalamin

**Daily Stack — stock formula path:**
4. Closest stock formula match? (Looking for: Zinc + D3 + B12 combo with Ashwagandha if possible)
5. Customisation options on stock formulas — can specific ingredients be swapped or upgraded (e.g. swapping cyanocobalamin for Methylcobalamin if their stock uses the cheaper form)?
6. Pricing per unit on stock vs custom path

**Joint & Recovery Collagen:**
7. Their existing collagen private-label range — formulations available?
8. How close to our spec (Hydrolysed Bovine 10g, UC-II 40mg, Vitamin C 80mg, MSM 500mg, Hyaluronic Acid 5mg)?

10. Sachet filling capability on collagen specifically — included in their range or separate?

11. Mechanics — do you fulfil direct to customer using our branding, or is the brand secondary?
12. Per-unit dropship pricing vs bulk-purchase pricing
13. Order capacity — do you handle 20 orders/day at launch + scaling to 100/day?

**Both products / commercial:**
